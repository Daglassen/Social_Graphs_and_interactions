"{{Infobox_character||image = Jambalaya.jpg\n|shows = ''[[Darkwing Duck]]''\n|voice = [[Michael Gough]]\n|personality = Scheming, treacherous, bungling, funny\n|appearance = Short, obese man, brown beard, blue overalls\n|occupation = Bayou criminal\n|alignment = Bad\n|goal = To plot crimes and commit them\n|home = [[St. Canard]]\n|friends = His pet [[Gumbo]]\n|family = Granny Whammy (grandmother)\n|minions = [[Gumbo]]\n|enemies = [[Darkwing Duck (character)|Darkwing Duck]], [[Launchpad McQuack]], [[Gosalyn Mallard]]\n|affiliations = The League of Barely-Remembered Supervillains\n|likes = Plotting crimes\n|dislikes = Gumbo screwing things up, being on his grandmother's bad side, Granny Whammy's magical wrath\n|powers = Strength\n|weapons = Fists\n|quote = \"That's Gumbo.\"\n||pets = Gumbo (alligator)\n|fate = Arrested with Gumbo}}'''Jambalaya Jake''' is a recurring minor villain from ''[[Darkwing Duck]]''.\n\n==Background==\nJambalaya Jake lived in the bayou along with his pet alligator [[Gumbo]] (who seems to be much smarter than him), but the two decide to move to the big city after hearing it was easy pickings. The two make their hideout in the sewers of [[St. Canard]], where they plot their crimes. Jake and Gumbo first appear in the episode \"[[Can't Bayou Love]].\" By using [[Launchpad McQuack]] as bait, they try and catch [[Darkwing Duck (character)|Darkwing Duck]]. But Darkwing shows them first hand that in the city it is not so \"easy pickings.\"\n\nJambalaya Jake and Gumbo reappear in the episode \"[[Double Darkwings]]\" in an attempt to get even with Darkwing. Jake is also helped by his granny, an old witch who makes potions for him to use in exchange for money. At the end of the second episode, the two end up arrested for trying to frame Darkwing Duck for their crimes.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Similar to Gumbo, his name comes from that of a Cajun cuisine.
