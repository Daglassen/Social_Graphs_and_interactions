They can spit out balls of [[Ice]] that break into bursts of cold air when they make contact with the ground.<ref name=\"Compendium\">{{Cite|The balls of ice they spit make them particularly troublesome, but exposure to fire will kill them instantly.|Hyrule Compendium|BotW}}</ref> They have a moderate amount of health at 288. Hitting an Ice-Breath Lizalfos with a [[Fire]] attack, such as with [[Fire Arrow]]s or a [[Flameblade]], evaporates it instantly.<ref name=\"Compendium\"/> They are mostly found in snowy areas, such as the northern [[Gerudo Highlands]] and the [[Hebra Mountains]].
