"{{Infobox character\n|image = Cobra.png\n|name = Cobra\n|shows = ''[[Timon & Pumbaa]]''\n|personality = Cunning, sneaky, intimidating, dangerous, monstrous, sadistic\n|appearance = Large black snake with cream yellow chest, yellow eyes, pointy fangs\n|alignment = Bad/Neutral\n|goal = To kidnap Tatiana and eat her\n|enemies = [[Timon]], [[Pumbaa]], [[Tatiana]]\n|likes = Eating its prey\n|dislikes = Starvation\n|weapons = Fangs, coils\n|fate = Fails to eat Timon and Tatiana and dies after accidentally falling into a lava pit|alias=Snake|home=His lava hole|status=Deceased}}The '''Cobra''' is a character from the ''[[Timon & Pumbaa]]'' TV series who appears as the main antagonist in the episode \"[[Once Upon a Timon]]\".\n\n==Background==\n===Personality===\nThe cobra is a dangerous threat to [[Meerkat Colony|Timon's colony]]. S/he is cunning and opportunistic, as s/he took advantage of [[Timon]]'s negligence to kidnap [[Tatiana]].\n\n===Physical appearance===\nThe cobra is a massive snake. S/he has a long black-scaled body, with a cream underbelly, red nostrils, and dark brown eyes with yellow sclerae.\n\n==Role in the series==\nWhen Timon leaves his guard post to prepare for his date with Tatiana, the cobra sees that the back gate is unprotected. While Timon is taking a shower, the cobra goes in the meerkat colony and kidnaps Tatiana, with everyone believing the princess is dead. When the [[Duke Meerkat]] returns, he banishes Timon for leaving his guard post.\n\nAfter Timon meets [[Pumbaa]], the two see that the cobra has still not eaten Tatiana. Timon tells Pumbaa that if he helps him rescue the princess, he will be accepted to his colony. The cobra then tries to eat Timon and Tatiana, but fails and met it's own end when the cobra accidentally falls into a lava pit and dies.\n\n==Trivia==\n*The Cobra is the first character in ''[[Timon & Pumbaa]]'' to die, the other being [[Ned the Elephant]].\n*His/her name means 'Snake' in Portuguese. \n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery>\nOUAT cobra.png\nOUAT cobra2.png\nOUAT Cobra3.png\nOUAT Cobra4.png\nOUAT cobra5.png\nOUAT cobra6.png\nOUAT cobra7.png\n</gallery>\n\n{{DEFAULTSORT:Cobra, The}}\n\n{{TheLionKingWiki}}\n{{The Lion King}}\n[[Category:Timon & Pumbaa characters]]\n[[Category:Snakes]]\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Silent characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Deceased characters]]\n[[Category:The Disney Afternoon characters]]\n[[Category:Neutral characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]"
