Silver Moblins are recurring Enemies in The Legend of Zelda series.[name reference needed]


== Characteristics ==


=== Breath of the Wild ===

Silver Moblins are tall monsters that can be found all over Hyrule Kingdom. Due to Calamity Ganon's magic, they are a powerful variant of Moblin, surpassing even Black Moblins, but are outclassed by Golden Moblins. They are armed with the strongest Moblin Weapons.[which?] When defeated, they may drop Moblin Horns, Moblin Fangs, Moblin Guts, Amber, Opal, Topaz, Rubies, Sapphires, and Diamonds.
