When approached, he will be lost in thought, thinking of the Great Fairy.<ref>{{Cite|Ahh... {{Color|BotW Blue|Great Fairy}}...|Toren|BotW}}</ref> When Link talks to him, he will snap out of it and tell Link of the Great Fairy [[Kaysa]].<ref>{{Cite|You've heard of the {{Color|BotW Blue|Great Fairy}}, right?
