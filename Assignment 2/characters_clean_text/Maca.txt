Maca is a character in Breath of the Wild.


== Biography ==
Maca is a Korok that lives in the Korok Forest.  Maca can be found outside the entrance of the Great Deku Tree, and provides Link with information about the forest and Master Sword.  Maca tells Link that the Master Sword may lose power if overused, but will regenerate over time. When Link asks about leaving the forest, Maca questions why he would want to leave this perfect place. Regardless, Maca tells Link that he can leave the woods by warping through the mouth of an Ogre Tree east from the sword pedestal.


== Trivia ==
Maca's leaf mask and appearance resemble Hollo from The Wind Waker.
