"{{Infobox character\n|image = Jacques Lebeau.jpg\n|films = ''[[Nikki, Wild Dog of the North]]''\n|actor = \u00c9mile Genest\n|inspiration = Jacques Lebeau from James Oliver Curwood's novel\n|personality = Evil, cruel, heartless, uncaring, selfish\n|alignment = Bad\n|goal = To turn Nikki into a killing dog (failed)\n|home = Canadian Northwest|minions = [[Makoki]] (formerly)\n|friends = [[Durante]]\n|enemies = [[Nikki]], [[Andre Dupas]], [[Makoki]]\n|likes = Money\n|dislikes = Animals\n|weapons = Knife, gun\n|fate = Accidentally stabs himself after Nikki attacks him while he tries to kill Andre}}'''Jacques Lebeau''' is the main antagonist in the [[1961]] live-action film ''Nikki, Wild Dog of the North''.\n\n==Role==\nAfter Nikki and [[Neewa]] were free from being tied to each other, Nikki encounters Lebeau and his former Native American minion, [[Makoki]] after his scent lead to them, thinking it was [[Andre Dupas|Andre]]. Lebeau tries to shoot Nikki after he howls in sadness, realizing that he wasn't his master and flees from him, heartbroken.\n\nLater, he tries several times to trap Nikki and turn him into a fighting dog. However, he was too smart to fall in for that and throws snow onto the traps to get the meat. This time, Lebeau poisons the meat. When Nikki is barely poisoned, Lebeau captures him and trains him to become a fighting dog. After Nikki's fight with a dog, Andre arrives and is pushed into the ring by Lebeau and Nikki remembers him. However, Lebeau ties Nikki to a cage and fights Andre until he kicks him in the chest. However, Lebeau refuses to accept defeat and tries to stab Andre. However, Makoki frees Nikki and he attacks Lebeau, causing him to accidentally stab himself in the chest.
