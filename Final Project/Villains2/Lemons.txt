They are made up of four different mobster families based on their models: Gremlins (''AMC Gremlin''), Pacers (''AMC Pacer''), Trunkovs (''ZAZ Zaporozhets'') and the Hugos (''Zastava Koral''). There is also one Z\u00fcndapp Janus. Because they are considered ugly and weak cars, the Lemons feel rejected, and reject the world in return and seek to further enrich themselves through the control of the largest oil deposits in the Cars world and the tarnishing of the reputations of alternative fuels.
