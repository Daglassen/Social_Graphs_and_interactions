Wabbin is a character in Breath of the Wild.


== Biography ==
Wabbin is a timid young man seeking love. He is found at the top of Tuft Mountain, near Lover's Pond. As part of the Side Quest A Gift of Nightshade, he wishes to speak to the Gerudo woman Perda, whom he has feelings for. He asks Link to deliver a Blue Nightshade to her, citing that he long ago decided he would give that flower to the girl of his dreams. As a reward for setting him up with Perda, Wabbin gives Link 20 rupees. His new girlfriend scolds him for his cheapness, and he gives Link another 100 rupees out of fear.
