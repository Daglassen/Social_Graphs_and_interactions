Olu is a character in Breath of the Wild.


== Biography ==
Olu is a Gerudo who can be found right outside of Hotel Oasis. Olu works for the hotel, clapping her hands and advertising the Hotel to passing visitors. If Link speaks to Olu, she encourages him to relax his body and mind at Hotel Oasis. Olu recommends the Hotel's spa plan and cites it as the number one way to feel relaxed and beautiful. Olu tells Link to go inside and ask Romah for more information.
