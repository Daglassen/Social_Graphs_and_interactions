"{{NeedImage}}'''Igor Drenkov''' was Bruce Banner's old lab assistant and a [[Marvel Comics]] character that appeared in ''[[Avengers Assemble]]'', voiced by [[Andr\u00e9 Sogliuzzo]]. He leads his group called the SteelCorps in a fight against [[Avengers|the Avengers]] at [[Stark Industries]] and uses a gamma energy-stealing weapon on [[Hulk]].
