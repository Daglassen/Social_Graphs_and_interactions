That skin will bring me much money.\"actor = Paul Onsongo|alias = The Shoe Guy (by Ted)|friends = [[Mr. Patel]], [[Nigel (Cheetah)|Nigel]]|status=Arrested}}'''Abdullah'''\u00a0or\u00a0'''Abdallah'''\u00a0is the secondary antagonist of Disney's [[1989]] live-action film\u00a0''[[Cheetah]]''. He wants to skin Duma and collect money for him and his friends by making her to race against greyhounds, because of Mr. Patel's uncle. He is the slayer of Duma's mother. However, their plan is foiled by\u00a0[[Ted and Susan Johnson|Ted, Susan]], and\u00a0[[Morogo]], the protagonists and deuteragonist of the film.\n== Role in the film ==\nAbdullah is first seen planning to capture Duma with Patel and Nigel. When the trio goes to the Johnsons' house, Patel sneaks into the house to steal\u00a0Ted's\u00a0cheetah whistle. After he got the whistle, they capture Duma.\n\nAbdullah decides to train her for running fast. When the Johnson family go to Patel's store, Ted recognizes the shoe marks on Abdullah's shoe and realizes that he killed Duma's mother and realizes that Patel has captured her, because of his mentioning of Duma being lost. He also realizes that Patel has stolen his whistle.\n\nWhen Abdullah captures\u00a0Susan, he tries to stab her until he catches Ted in the act. After he puts Ted and Susan in a cage, they leave for Nairobi. However,\u00a0Morogo\u00a0frees Ted and Susan from their cage and go after the trio.\n\nWhen Abdullah tries to prevent her from winning, Ted snatches his whistle from a cop and blows the whistle and makes Duma go faster and reach the finish line.
