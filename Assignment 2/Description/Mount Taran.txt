There are three pedestals for orbs and a stone tablet that instructs Link on how to complete the shrine quest [[The Three Giant Brothers]]<ref>{{Cite|The {{Color|BotW Red|ancient orbs}} guarded by the giants of Mount Taran lead to the {{Color|BotW Red|shine}}|Stone Tablet|BotW}}</ref>.
