Piaffe is a character in Breath of the Wild.


== Biography ==
Piaffe is the manager of the Gerudo Canyon Stable in the Gerudo Highlands. He offers Link the ability to board his Horse at the Stable. As the Stable doubles as an Inn, Piaffe also offers Link a chance to rest in a bed inside the Stable and recover his Hearts.
