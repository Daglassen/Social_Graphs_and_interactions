"{{Infobox character\n|name=Baron Blitz\n|shows=''[[Teamo Supremo]]''\n|voice=[[Maurice LaMarche]]\n|personality=Insidious and cruel\n|occupation=Dictator\n|goal=To take over the State\n|minions=Wax sculptures, bronze statues, audio-animatronics\n|enemies=Teamo Supremo, Governor Kevin, [[The Chief]]\n|fate=Always get sent back to jail\n|quote=\"G-g-g-g-get them!\"\n\"Once You Have Bought Them In I Will Own Evreything Ount Then It Will be Welcome To Blitzelvania Mwahahahahhahaha\"!\n|image = Baron_Blitz.jpg|alignment = Bad|inspiration = [[Stitch]]\n[[Dracula Duck]]}}'''Baron Blitz''' is one of the recurring villains of ''[[Teamo Supremo]]'', and notably the first villain the series' titular characters ever went up against. He is a short, [[Napoleon Bonaparte|Napoleonic]] dictator with blue skin and a German accent who frequently schemes to take over the state. His origins are never explained.\n\nBlitz usually made attempts to take over the State by stealing money in order to gain power. In his last appearance, his goal expands to world domination. However, he always fails because of his continuous losses to Teamo. His minions usually consist of inanimate objects which he has brought to life by use of remote control, including wax statues, bronze football players, and audio-animatronics.\n\nAlong with [[the Birthday Bandit]], Blitz was the most frequently recurring of the ''Teamo Supremo'' villains, appearing in five episodes -
