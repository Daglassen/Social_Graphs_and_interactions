Ever since he left the circus, a new Trick Shot has taken his place.\u00a0He and [[Hawkeye]] battled it out in combat in the episode \"Crime and Circuses\".\u00a0Hawkeye was able to overpower him, and took his costume to fool the Ring Master and Princess Python.\n\n==Trivia==\n*In the comics, there have been two archers known as Trick Shot. The first of which was '''Buck Chislom''', who was part of the Circus Hawkeye grew up in and mentored Hawkeye into mastering the bow and arrow, he eventually developed cancer and became a supervillain, though aided Hawkeye in a number of scenarios.
