In the episode \"[[St. Olga's Reform School for Wayward Princesses (episode)|St. Olga's Reform School for Wayward Princesses]]\", a teacher is trying to teach her how to be gentle and kind with birds, but she just keeps eating them, prompting the teacher to repeat the lesson over and over.\n\nIn the episode \"[[Princess Turdina]]\", she was captured by [[Meteora Butterfly]] and drained of her youth.
