The rock chunks that they launch at {{Term|BotW|Link}} will always be coated in ice.\n\n{{Term|BotW|Link}} can defeat a {{Term|BotW|Frost Talus}} the same way as every other variant of {{Term|BotW|Stone Talus|display=Talus}}, inflicting damage to their {{Plural|BotW|Ore Deposit|link}} through melee attacks, firing {{Plural|BotW|Arrow|link}}, or by using {{Term|BotW|Urbosa's Fury|link}}.\n\nBy defeating all Taluses in {{Term|BotW|Hyrule Kingdom|link}}, including Frost Taluses, Link will receive the [[Medal of Honor: Talus]] from [[Kilton]].
