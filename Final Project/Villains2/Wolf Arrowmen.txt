They serve as henchmen, alongside the [[Rhino Guards]], to [[Prince John]] and the [[Sheriff of Nottingham]].\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[Robin Hood (film)|Robin Hood]]''===\nThe Wolf Arrowmen are first seen during the film's opening credits. Later, during the song \"[[Oo-De-Lally]]\" (sung by [[Alan-A-Dale]]), they are seen trying to kill [[Robin Hood (character)|Robin Hood]] and [[Little John]]\u00a0after the two outlaws were seen playing\u00a0and goofing off\u00a0in a pond. However, Robin Hood and Little John manage to escape the Wolf Archers.\n\nThe Wolf Arrowmen are later seen chasing after the innocent citizens of [[Nottingham]]\u00a0after an archery tournament went awry, due to [[Prince John]] being thwarted by Little John when the former attempted to behead Robin Hood after hearing him talk about [[King Richard]]\u00a0being the rightful king.\u00a0A number of Wolf Archers\u00a0can be\u00a0seen\u00a0relentlessly pursuing [[Maid Marian]].\u00a0She calls out for Robin Hood's help and is heroically rescued by him. He is then seen fighting off a few of the Wolf Arrowmen with a sword.\n\nThe\u00a0Wolf Arrowmen\u00a0are last seen chasing the citizens of Nottingham when they attempt to escape from jail. Later, under Prince John's orders, several Wolf Archers attempt to kill Robin Hood by firing their arrows at him, but he manages to flee to safety. It is unknown what happens to them after [[King Richard]] returns, they either got imprisoned or changed their alliance to him.\n\n===''[[Mickey Mouse (TV series)|Mickey Mouse]]''===\nThe Wolf Arrowmen make a cameo in the Season 4 episode \"[[Roll 'em]]\" as actors who invade the castle of the [[The King (Brave Little Tailor)|King]] right before [[Mickey Mouse|Mickey]] interrupts the filming.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\" spacing=\"small\" widths=\"185\">\nRobin-hood-disneyscreencaps.com-364-1-.jpg\nRobin-hood-disneyscreencaps.com-379-1-.jpg\nRobin-hood-disneyscreencaps.com-5338-1-.jpg\nRobin-hood-disneyscreencaps.com-9012-1-.jpg\nWolfArrowmenRollEm.jpeg|Cameo in ''[[Mickey Mouse (TV series)|Mickey Mouse]]''\nDVG Wolf Archers.png\n</gallery>\n\n{{Robin Hood}}\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Robin Hood characters]]\n[[Category:Wolves]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Archers]]\n[[Category:Henchmen]]\n[[Category:Anthropomorphic characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Silent characters]]\n[[Category:Character groups]]\n[[Category:Characters in the Disney animated features canon]]\n[[Category:Mickey Mouse (TV series) characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]"
