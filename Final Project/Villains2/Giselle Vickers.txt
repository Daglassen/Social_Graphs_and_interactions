She is\u00a0Douglas' former colleague, and is the owner of\u00a0Giselle's Robotics Company. She's a big Hollywood filmmaker, making the\u00a0''Zombie Truck Stop''\u00a0movie, and wanted to make a movie about [[Adam Davenport|Adam]], [[Bree Davenport|Bree]] and [[Chase Davenport|Chase]], featuring actor\u00a0Troy West, whom is later revealed to be an\u00a0android. However, this is later revealed to be a cover up to create an\u00a0android army, to replace the Bionic Superheroes. Adam, Bree, Chase,\u00a0Leo\u00a0and Douglas put an end to her operation by destroying all of her androids, but she escaped, resurrecting\u00a0Marcus.
