"{{Infobox character\n|shows = ''[[Adventures of the Gummi Bears]]''\n|image = Flint Shrubwood.png\n|name= Flint Shrubwood\n|voice = [[Corey Burton]] \n|inspiration = [[Clint Eastwood]] in the ''[[Wikipedia:Dollars Trilogy|Dollars Trilogy]]''\n|likes =Being handsomely rewarded \n|powers = \n|paraphernalia = Dagger, boomerang gun\n|dislikes = Not getting paid\n|home = \n|occupation = Mercenary & Bounty Hunter\n}}\n'''Flint Shrubwood''' is an antagonist in ''[[Adventures of the Gummi Bears]]''. He appears in the episode \"[[For a Few Sovereigns More]]\".\n\nThe best bounty hunter in the land, he can track anything or anybody, even [[Gummi Bears]].\n\nShrubwood is hired by [[Duke Igthorn]], to capture a Gummi Bear. However, when Igthorn refuses to pay him for the capture of [[Cubbi Gummi|Cubbi]], he takes the Duke for ransom.\n\nAfter Igthorn and Cubbi escape, Shrubwood hunts them, until he manages to acquire the pay that the Duke had refused him. Having acquired the gold, he lets both Igthorn and Cubbi go.\n\n==Trivia==\n*The name Flint Shrubwood is a parody of the name Clint Eastwood.\n*The character is a parody of Clint Eastwood's bounty hunter character in the ''[[Wikipedia:Dollars Trilogy|Dollars Trilogy]]''.\n*The line \"Go ahead, take my pay\", is a parody of the famous line \"Go ahead, make my day\", said by Eastwood in the ''Dirty Harry'' movie ''Sudden Impact''.\n*Corey Burton even does a Clint Eastwood impersonation when voicing the character.\n*Shrubwood is one of a relatively small group of humans who find out about the existence of Gummi Bears during the course of the series.
