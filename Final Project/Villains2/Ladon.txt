Out of all her children Ladon is the only one who bares a physical resemblance to both his parents.\n\nLadon also appears in ''[[Once Upon a Time]]''. He was represented by one of Hercules' twelve medals when telling his heroic past to an teenage Snow White, depicted like his mythic description.\n\n==Trivia==\n*In the original myth, Ladon was an dragon/snake with one head (or 100 in others versions), recruited by Hera for to control the Hesperides Garden and the Golden Apples Tree.
