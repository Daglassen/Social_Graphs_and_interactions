They have vast resources, a large number of high-tech gadgets, and extensive knowledge of magic and cosmic forces.\n\nThe Hand was formed centuries ago, in feudal Japan, and continued to expand to this very day.\n\nThe Hand are also Yakuza, meaning they control the organized crime in Japan and the Asian communities of the U.S.A.\n\nThe former leader was Nobu Yoshioka, who wanted to take over New York with the help of a super soldier known as Black Sky.
