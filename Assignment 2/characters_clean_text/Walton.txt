Walton is a character in Breath of the Wild.


== Biography ==

Walton can be found outside atop of the Great Deku Tree in the Korok Forest. When spoken with, Walton gives Link the "Riddles of Hyrule" Side Quest.


== Trivia ==
Walton's leaf mask and appearance resemble Drona from The Wind Waker.
