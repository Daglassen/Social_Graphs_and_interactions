"[[File:Pansy.jpg|thumb|250px|Pantsy Gonzalez]]\n'''Pantsy''' is a character and antagonist from ''[[Kick Buttowski: Suburban Daredevil]]''.\n\n==History==\nPantsy is one of the ushers at the local movie theater. He always has his 3D glasses on, and when not in uniform wears a simple hoodie. He only appears in a few episodes, and is always assisting [[Brad Buttowski|Brad]] when he does. He is a son of the 2nd assistant security guard at Mellowbrook Mall. He is known for taking his job at the movie theatre far too seriously, kicking out practically everyone for minor infractions (including his younger brother [[Mouth]] and Kick).\n\nHe is a 15-year-old sidekick of Brad.
