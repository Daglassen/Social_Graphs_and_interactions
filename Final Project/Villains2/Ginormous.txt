"[[File:Ginormous_Artwork.jpg|thumb]]\n'''Ginormous''' is a monster truck, who first appears in [[Cars (video game)|''Cars: The Video Game'']]. He came to Radiator Springs with his friends Count Spatula and The Crippler to challenge Lightning to a monster truck race.\nHe is a monster truck that is painted gray, with a darker shade of gray towards his rear. He has two sets of exhaust pipes, one on each side of his doors, as well as a gray bumper, a chrome grille and silver \"W\" spokes on his tires.\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Pixar characters]]\n[[Category:Pixar villains]]\n[[Category:Cars characters]]\n[[Category:Characters in video games]]"
