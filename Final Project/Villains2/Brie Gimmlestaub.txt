"[[File:Bandicam_2013-07-30_18-35-41-498.jpg|thumb|250px]]'''Brie Gimmlestaub''' is a minor character of [[Disney Channel Original Series|Disney Channel's Original Series]],\u00a0''[[Dog With a Blog]]''. She is the daughter of Pasadena's Mayor. She is portrayed by [[Dara Sisterhen]].\n\n==Biography==\nBrie was one of the members of the cheerleader squad that befriended [[Avery Jennings|Avery]] in Guess Who's a Cheerleader. She also disliked [[Lindsay]], like the other cheerleaders. She attended the sleepover that [[Ellen Jennings|Ellen]] and Avery held.\n\nIn ''Avery's Wild Party'', it is revealed that Brie's mother is the mayor of Pasadena, and Avery throws a party for her in hopes of landing an internship with her mother. Throughout the episode, she is sarcastic and a bit of a diva, doing things her way, which to her is the only way.\n\n==Personality==\nBrie is portrayed as very sarcastic and mean to others, although she appears to have lots of friends, most likely because her mother is the mayor.
