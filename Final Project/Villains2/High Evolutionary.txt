High Evolutionary was created by [[Stan Lee]] and [[Jack Kirby]].\n\n==History==\nHe is a mad scientist obsessed with creating the perfect race of beings through evolution. His constant genetic experiments on himself gave him the ability to warp reality at will, the ability to grow to giant size, the ability to fire energy beams from his hands, telepathy, telekinesis, the ability to see the future, and the ability to teleport to any place in the universe.
