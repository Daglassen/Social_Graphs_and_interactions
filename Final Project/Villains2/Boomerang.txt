He throws boomerangs at opponents. He also has jet boots so he can fly.\n\nBoomerang was created by\u00a0[[Stan Lee]]\u00a0and\u00a0[[Jack Kirby]].\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[Ultimate Spider-Man]]''===\n[[Mesmero]] mind-controls Boomerang into accompanying [[Grizzly]] and [[Shocker]] into attacking Spider-Man, Power Man, and Squirrel Girl. Mesmero was forced by Spider-Man to free Boomerang and the other villains from his mind-control. Boomerang, Mesmero, and Shocker were webbed up by Spider-Man and left for the police.
