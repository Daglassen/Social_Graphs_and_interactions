The [[Eye of Agamotto]] was named after him.\n\n==Appearances==\n===[[Marvel Cinematic Universe]]===\n====''[[Doctor Strange (film)|Doctor Strange]]''====\nAgamotto is mentioned throughout the film. He was the founder of the [[Masters of the Mystic Arts]] and their three sanctuaries thousands of years ago, and created the Eye of Agamotto from the [[Infinity Stones|Time Stone]] as his personal item. At some unknown point, he was succeeded by another sorcerer as the Masters' leader, beginning a series of successions that led to the [[Ancient One]], and then [[Doctor Strange]].\n\n===[[Marvel Animation]]===\n====''[[Avengers Assemble]]''====\nAgamotto appears as the former Sorcerer Supreme for Earth.\u00a0Before the series began, he was captured, his eye was removed and sealed away in another dimension.
