"{{Species Infobox\n|image = [[File:Harpy Hercules.png|thumb]]\n|films=''[[Hercules (film)|Hercules]]''|games=''[[Hercules (video game)|Hercules]]''|inspiration=[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Harpy Harpies]|behaviour=Hostile|abilities=Flight|name=Harpies}}'''Harpies''' are minor antagonists in the [[1997]] film ''[[Hercules (film)|Hercules]]'' and in the 1997 [[Hercules (video game)|video game of the same name]].\n\n==Appearances==\n===[[Hercules (film)|''Hercules'']]===\nIn the film, a giant Harpy appears as one of the many enemies that Hercules needs to fight. It tries to swoop down on him from the sky, but it is quickly defeated and put into a cage. \n\n===[[Hercules (video game)|''Hercules'' Video Game]]===\nIn the video game, they serve as a group of minor antagonists that Hercules needs to fight off in the city. They appear sitting in the background, and once he appears , they begin flying above him. Hercules needs to use attacks that fire up into the air in order to defeat them. Harpies again appear during one of the final segments while Hercules is flying on his [[Pegasus]]. Swarms of them try to attack Hercules, and they need to be defeated in a similar fashion to the way that they were in earlier levels. \n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\" captionalign=\"center\">\nHercules Harpy Defeated.png|A Harpy being defeated in the Hercules film. \nHarpies SNES.png|Harpies in the SNES game. \n</gallery>\n\n==Trivia==\n*Other than their design, the Harpy and the Harpies from the video game have many differences. \n**In the film the Harpy was twice the size of Hercules, while in the video game they were twice as small. In the movie, Hercules only fought one while in the video game he fought multiple.
