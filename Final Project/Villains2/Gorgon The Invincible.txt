Gorgon has the abilities to look like a [[Bandersnatch]].\n\n==Background==\nGorgon was one of the previous [[Dark One|Dark Ones]], he stabbed a Dark One and gains the power of the Dark One and the [[Dark One's Dagger|Dark One Dagger]], becoming Gorgon the Invincible. he began to shapeshifted into a fire-breathing Bandersnatch, which is unknown that that is a way of a Bandersnatch. Gorgon seeks to steal the [[Sorcerer Hat|Sorcerer's Hat]], but failed, but in fact he dos not want to give up his power. His fate was ended after he was stabbed by [[Zoso]] who now becomes the Dark One.<br />\n\n==Role in the series==\n\n===Before the Third Curse===\nAfter becoming the Dark One, [[Emma Swan]] sees the Darkness in the forms of [[Rumplestiltskin|Rumpelstiltskin]] and Gorgon for it is all Dark Ones, but the Darkness prefers to look like Rumple.\n\n===After the Third Curse===\nGorgon is present with all past Dark Ones lead by [[Nimue (character)|Nimue]], appearing in [[Emma Swan|Emma]]'s head to watch her reunite [[Sword in the Stone (object)|Excalibur]] with the [[Dark One's Dagger]].\n\nAfter [[Captain Hook|Hook]] creates a portal to the [[Underworld]], Gorgon, along with the other Dark Ones all return in their physical form to [[Storybrooke]] to snuff out the light.\n\n[[Nimue (character)|Nimue]], Zoso, Gorgon and the Dark Ones marked the people in Storybrooke so they can take their place in the Underworld, just has it was the time, Hook absorbs Nimue, Gorgon and all the Dark Ones into Excalibur and Emma kills Killian and destroys Excalibur and the Darkness.
