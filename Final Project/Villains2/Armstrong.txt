"{{Infobox character\n|image = ArmVacuum.jpg\n|shows = ''[[DuckTales]]''<br>[[DuckTales (2017 series)|''DuckTales'' (2017)]] (cameo)\n|voice = [[Peter Cullen]]\n|appearance = Yellow robot, flexible arms, light bulbs for his nose and ears\n|occupation = Servant\n|home = [[Duckburg]]\n|fate = Gets doused with water and short circuits\n|alignment = Good, later bad\n|powers = Super strength, super extending arms, can control anything mechanical}}'''Armstrong''' is a robot created by [[Gyro Gearloose]], which appeared in the ''[[DuckTales]]'' [[Armstrong (episode)|episode of the same name]].\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[DuckTales]]''===\nArmstrong is introduced as Gyro's latest invention. He is helpful for a while, until he takes over [[Launchpad McQuack|Launchpad]] and [[Duckworth]]'s jobs. Then he turns bad, steals Scrooge's money and holds him and Gyro hostage. He was defeated when water from Launchpad's plane was dumped on him, causing him to short-circuit and fall to pieces.\n===[[DuckTales (2017 series)|''DuckTales'' (2017 series)]]===\nIn the first episode \"[[Woo-oo!]]\", Armstrong's head is shown to be kept inside Scrooge's garage.
