"[[File:Josh_(The_Weekenders).jpg|thumb|250px]]'''Josh''' (voiced by [[Carlos Alazraqui]])<ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/The-Weekenders/Josh/</ref> is a secondary character from the Disney animated series [[The Weekenders|''The Weekenders'']]. He is the local bully and is very fond of getting into fights with [[Carver Descartes|Carver]] because of his claims to be tough. Josh seems to be secretly a coward, as he was nervous when Carver actually dared to stand up to him.
