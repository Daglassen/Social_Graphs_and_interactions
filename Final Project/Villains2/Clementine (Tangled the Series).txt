"\n\n'''Clementine''' is a character in ''[[Tangled: The Series]]''.\n\n{{Infobox character\n|image = [[File:Clementine Rapunzel's Tangled Adventure.jpg|thumb]]\n|shows= ''[[Tangled: The Series]]''\n|voice= [[Betsy Sodaro]]\n|likes=Bringing form chaos and annihilation to Corona\n|dislikes=The royal family and people of Corona\n|powers=Various magics\n|affiliations=[[Separatists of Saporia]]}}\n\n==Background==\nClementine appears alongside the Separatists of Saporia in \"[[Rapunzel's Return]]\". When the king and queen had their memories erased by her, they lay siege to Corona and forced its citizens to mine for crystals. After Varian's plan to erase all the memories with the Quirineon failed, Clementine suggested that they would use it to reduce Corona to ashes.
