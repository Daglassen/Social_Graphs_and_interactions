"{{Infobox character\n|name = Magma\n|image = Magma19-1-.jpg\n|shows = ''[[Aladdin (TV series)|Aladdin]]''\n|voice = [[Tone L\u014dc]]\n|alias = The Lord of Volcanoes\n|personality = Grumpy, evil, mean, hot-tempered, careless, somewhat obedient \n|appearance = Fire elemental ifrit made of molten rock\n|occupation = \n|alignment = Bad\n|goal = To do Abis Mal's bidding until he has fulfilled his request\n|home = Volcanoes\n|friends = [[Abis Mal]] and [[Haroud Hazi Bin]]\n|enemies = [[Aladdin (character)|Aladdin]] and his friends\n|likes = Heat, fire, lava, and getting what he wants for Abis Mal\n|dislikes = Frigid lands and not getting what he wants for Abis Mal\n|powers = Earth temperature control\n|fate = Launched into the sun after falling down a deep well\n|quote = \"Then all of Agrabah will suffer the wrath of Magma!\"\n}}'''Magma''' is a fire elemental ifrit who appears in the [[Aladdin (TV series)|''Aladdin'' TV series]] episode \"[[Smolder and Wiser]]\".\n\n==Role in the series==\nMagma is a fire elemental ifrit with the power to control Earth's temperature. Known as the Lord of Volcanoes, he is summoned by lighting the Candle of Magma. [[Abis Mal]] calls for him so he will force the [[Sultan]] to hand over [[Agrabah]] to the thief. When the Sultan refuses, Magma heats up the city ground temperature at the point that water vaporizes and wooden carts spontaneously explode. Eventually, the ifrit is defeated when he falls down a deep well. The resulting steam pressure launches him into the sun. [[Genie]] comments that Magma has finally found somewhere warm enough.\n\n== Appearance ==\nIn his normal form, Magma is bipedal and is covered with black molten rock.
