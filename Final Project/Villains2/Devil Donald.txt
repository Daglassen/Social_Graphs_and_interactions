"{{Infobox character\n|image = donaldsdevil.jpg\n|name = Devil Donald\n|films = ''[[Flubber]]'' (cameo)\n|shorts = ''[[Donald's Better Self]]<br>[[Donald's Decision]]''\n|shows = ''[[Legend of the Three Caballeros]]''\n|voice = Don Brodie<br>[[Kevin Michael Richardson]] (''Legend of the Three Caballeros'')\n|enemies = [[Angel Donald]]\n|likes = Pranks, mayhem, chaos\n|dislikes = Angel Donald interfering, morals}}'''Devil Donald''' is the bad side of [[Donald Duck]]'s conscience, personified as a shiftless and practical joke playing devil.\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[Donald's Better Self]]''===\nDonald's bad side makes his first appearance in ''Donald's Better Self''. Donald's good side persuades Donald to go to school while his bad side tempts him to go fishing. He forces Donald to smoke and making him sick until he gets into a fight with Angel Donald and got defeated.\n\n===''[[Donald's Decision]]''===\nIn this wartime retread of the latter, the Devil encourages Donald to be stingy and that war bonds are a scam, while Donald's Angel says that he'll be helping the war effort by making a loan to the US government.\n\n===''[[Legend of the Three Caballeros]]''===\nDevil Donald appears resuming the role of Donald's bad side. Humorously, he appears as both Donald's good and bad sides in \"[[Labyrinth and Repeat]]\". In \"[[Mt. Fuji Whiz]]\", Devil Donald turns out to be the ruler of the Underworld. He berates [[Baron Von Sheldgoose]] for letting the Three Caballeros escape the Underworld and being a terrible manager and fires him. In \"[[Shangri-La-Di-Da]]\", he appears as part of Donald's vision about his inability to control his anger.\n\n===Other appearances===\nDevil Donald was briefly seen on [[Weebo]] the Robot's monitor in the live-action film ''[[Flubber]]'' via archive footage from his debut short.\n\nIn ''[[Mickey's House of Villains]]'', Donald has a very similar devil costume.\n\nIn ''[[DuckTales (2017 series)|DuckTales]]'' the 2017 series, Della wore a devil outfit similar to her brother's in \"[[The Trickening!]]\".\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\" widths=\"185\">\nBetter-self-model-sheet-600.jpg\n26707.jpg\n26709.jpg\n26711.jpg\nDonald sleeping with his devil self.png\n1938-ange-3.jpg\nTumblr n5r938wZw01qhcrb0o1 1280.jpg\nBETTER SELF.png\n33607.jpg\nFlubberDonaldDevil.jpg|His cameo appearance in ''[[Flubber]]''\nWDCC Donald's Better Self Devil Donald.jpg|Devil Donald in the [[Walt Disney Classics Collection]]\nDonald's Devil and Angel.jpg\n</gallery>\n{{Mickey Mouse & Friends}}\n[[sr-el:Pajin \u0111avo]]\n[[Category:Donald Duck universe characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Ducks]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Demons]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Alternate Forms]]\n[[Category:The Three Caballeros characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]"
