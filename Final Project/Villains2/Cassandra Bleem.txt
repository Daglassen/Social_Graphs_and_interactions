"{{Dablink|the character Cassandra from [[Disney's Doug|Doug]]|other characters|[[Cassandra]]}}\n[[File:Cassandra Bleem.jpg|thumb]]\n'''Cassandra Bleem''' is a minor guest character of Disney's ''[[Disney's Doug|Doug]]''. She is voiced by Melissa Greenspan.\n\n==Biography==\nShe a superficial girl an a eighth grader and friends who appears in \"[[Doug's Older Woman]]\" where [[Doug Funnie]] asked her on a date. However, during the date, Doug heard her say hurtful and mean things about Patti and realized she was bad news. Doug realized she only asked him out so he could make a fool of himself in front of her friends. She is by far, the most antagonistic character on the show.
