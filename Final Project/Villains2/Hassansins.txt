"{{Infobox character\n|image = Hassansins.jpg\n|films = ''[[Prince of Persia: The Sands of Time]]''\n|alias = Assassins\n|occupation = Warriors\n|friends = [[Nizam]]\n|enemies = [[Prince Dastan]], [[Princess Tamina]]}}The '''Hassansins''' are a group of feared Persian warriors from ''[[Prince of Persia: The Sands of Time]]''. They\u00a0once served the kings of Persia until [[King Sharaman]] ordered them disbanded. Hassansins fight with no remorse, each one able to cause maximum damage by wielding ferocious weapons.\n\n==Background==\nThe order of Hassansins were to be dissolved by the king, but they were kept together and hidden by [[Nizam]], who hired the group to take down [[Prince Dastan]] and\u00a0[[Princess Tamina]] and steal the Dagger of Time from the two of them.\n\n==Members==\n*'''Hassansin Leader''': He is portrayed by G\u00edsli \u00d6rn Gar\u00f0arsson\n*'''Hassansin Porcupine''': He is portrayed by Claudio Pacifico\n*'''Hassansin Whip Man''': He is portrayed by Thomas DuPont\n*'''Hassansin Giant Scimitar''': He is portrayed by Dave Pope\n*'''Hassansin Double-Bladed Halberd''': He is portrayed by Domonkos Pardanyi\n*'''Hassansin Long Razor''': He is portrayed by Massimiliano Ubaldi\n*'''Hassansin Grenade Man''': He is portrayed by Vladimir Furdick\n\n==Trivia==\n*The word \"Hassassin\", would later be more commonly be known as Assassin.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\">\nprince-of-persia-391.jpg\nprince-of-persia-453.jpg\nprince-of-persia-486.jpg\nprinceof-persia-488.jpg\nprinceofpersia503.jpg\nprince-of-persia-539.jpg\nprince-of-persia-615.jpg\n</gallery>\n\n{{CharacterStub}}\n[[Category:Live-action characters]]\n[[Category:Prince of Persia: The Sands of Time characters]]\n[[Category:Character groups]]\n[[Category:Soldiers]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Character stubs]]\n[[Category:Persian characters]]\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Live-action villains]]\n[[Category:Mercenaries]]\n[[Category:Henchmen]]\n[[Category:Swordsmen]]\n[[Category:Armies]]"
