ZO is last seen getting stitched up and returning to his planet after observing Donald (all stitched up as well) having [[Daisy Duck|Daisy]] disbelieve his adventure story; including his encounter with ZO plus many other unexpected events.\n\n===Return of the T-Squad===\nZO plans to get his revenge on Donald Duck after their last encounter. He kidnaps Donald, which encourages his nephews: [[Huey, Dewey, and Louie]] to return to their alternate hero forms known as the T-Squad. After ZO keeps the T-Squad occupied with disasters on Earth, he transports Donald to his planet where he is sentenced underground to be sacrificed to a giant man-eating noodle. When the T-Squad returns, ZO brings out a [[Robo-bliterator|giant robot warrior]] for them to face. After the robot overpowers the T-Squad with their weakness: Broccoli, the young Ducks manage to take control of it from the inside. After the Ducks manage to save their uncle from being eaten by the noodle; using the robot, ZO runs away scared from the robot while vowing never \"to meddle in Earth's business again.\"\n\n==Personality==\nZO appears to tyrannical, ruthless, evil, and mean throughout the episodes.\n\n==Appearance==\nZO is an alien with yellow eyes, purple skin, and elephant-like face wearing a blue suit, hat, gloves, and black shoes.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery>\nQuack Pack Episode 9 The Late Donald Duck - YouTube12.jpg|ZO as he appeared in ''The Late Donald Duck''\nQuack Pack Episode 9 The Late Donald Duck - YouTube122.jpg|ZO's first defeat\nQuack Pack Episode 12 Return of the T Squad - YouTube144.jpg|ZO's second defeat\n</gallery>\n{{Quack Pack}}\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Quack Pack characters]]\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Lords]]\n[[Category:Aliens]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:The Disney Afternoon characters]]\n[[Category:Donald Duck universe characters]]"
