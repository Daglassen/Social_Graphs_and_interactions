She appears in \"[[Fu Dog Takes a Walk]]\".\n==History==\nShe is a dog catcher who is, ironically,\u00a0allergic to dogs. While [[Fu Dog]] is on his way to the baseball game, Ogelvy catches him. She brings him to a factory where she captures other dogs to destroy all dog breeds. She wants to do this to create a remedy to get rid of allergies.
