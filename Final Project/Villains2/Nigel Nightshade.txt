He plans to steal the golden key of [[Christopher Columbus]]' recently discovered fourth fictional ship, the \"Snookie\".\n\n== Personality ==\nLike all thieves, Nigel Nightshade is known to be conniving, sneaky, clever, lying, greedy, selfish, tricky, deceiving, mean, cunning, and slick in many ways when it comes to stealing and tricking people to gain their trust and get exactly what he's after.\n== Appearance ==\nNigel Nightshade appears to be a short man with black hair wearing a gray suit, red bow tie, and black shoes.\n== Trivia ==\n* He might be inspired by ''The Princess Bride'' character\u00a0Vizinni (played by [[Wallace Shawn]]), for they're alike in many ways.\n\n== Gallery ==\n<gallery>\nNigelcard.jpg|Nigel's Villain Card\nNigelfrench.jpg|Nigel's French Interpol Agent LeFort disguise\nNigeltux.jpg|Nigel dressed like Dr. Jekyll\nNigelhyde.jpg|Nigel as Mr. Hyde\nNigelpharaoh.jpg|Nigel dressed as a Pharaoh\nNigelsanta.jpg|Nigel dressed as [[Santa Claus]]\nNigelcowboy.jpg|Nigel dressed as a cowboy\nNigelarab.jpg|Nigel dressed as an Arab\nKenty2.jpg\nNigeldefeat.jpg|Nigel Nightshade's defeat\n</gallery>\n\n{{DEFAULTSORT:Nightshade, Nigel}}\n\n{{CharacterStub}}\n{{Quack Pack}}\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Quack Pack characters]]\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Thieves]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Donald Duck universe characters]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:The Disney Afternoon characters]]\n[[Category:Characters who break the Fourth Wall]]"
