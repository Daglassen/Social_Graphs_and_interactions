"{{Group Infobox\n|image = ID9 seeker droid.png\n|shows = ''[[Star Wars Rebels]]''\n|games = ''[[Star Wars Battlefront II]]''\n|alias = Pet\n|occupation = Probe droid\n|affiliation = [[Galactic Empire]]<br>[[Inquisitorius]]\n|goal = |status=Unknown}}{{Quote|My pet told me you were here.|Seventh Sister}}\nThe '''ID9 Seeker Droid''' (also referred as '''Parrot Droids''') is a small Imperial probe droid that appears in the second season of ''[[Star Wars Rebels]]''.\n\nThey may be small in size, but ID9 seeker droids are dangerous and deadly when used for nefarious purposes. Resembling probe droids, ID9 seekers had a half-hemisphere dome with a threatening red eye. They could hover and crawl on their pincers, imitate other droids, and were outfitted with electro-shock prods that could incapacitate mechanical beings and harm organic lifeforms.
