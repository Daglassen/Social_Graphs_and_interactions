Offrak is a character in Breath of the Wild.


== Biography ==
Offrak is a Goron child who can be found rolling around Goron City. Offrak observes that since Link is a Hylian, he needs Fireproof Elixirs to withstand the heat. He offers to sell him one for 60 Rupees. Offrak is business savvy, and gets very upset if Link refuses his offer.
During the night, Offrak can be found at the Protein Palace, falling asleep late at night. He can be woken up to buy a Fireproof Elixir.
