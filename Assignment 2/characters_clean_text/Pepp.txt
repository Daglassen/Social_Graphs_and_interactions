Pepp is a character in Breath of the Wild.


== Biography ==
Pepp can be found inside the Great Deku Tree, specifically inside the Navel, in the Korok Forest.  Pepp made a bed of leaves for Link, where Link can sleep for free to pass time or recover hearts.  Pepp often falls asleep while waiting for Link.


== Trivia ==
Pepp's leaf mask and appearance resemble Aldo from The Wind Waker.
