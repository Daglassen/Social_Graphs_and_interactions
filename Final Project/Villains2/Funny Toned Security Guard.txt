"[[File:Vlcsnap-2011-12-05-13h26m23s47.png|thumb|250px]]\n'''Funny Toned Security Guard''' is a character from  ''[[The Proud Family]]''.\n\n==History==\nHe is a man that works for [[Wizard Kelly]]. He has an accent similar to that of\u00a0[[Arnold Schwarzenegger]]. He once worked at a salon asking [[Trudy Proud|Trudy]] and [[Sunset Boulevardez|Sunset]] which color nail polish they would prefer. He later appears preventing [[Oscar Proud|Oscar]] from entering Wizard Kelly Mansion without a ticket. At the Wizard Kelly Auditorium, he inspects the tickets that [[Dijonay Jones|Dijonay]], [[Zoey Howzer|Zoey]], and [[LaCienega Boulevardez|LaCienega]] got from [[The Gross Sisters]] and declares them fake and throws them out of the building.\n\nHe also worked at Wizard Kelly Studios, where he asks [[Oscar Proud]] for a Studio pass.\n{{CharacterStub}}\n\n{{The Proud Family}}\n[[Category:The Proud Family characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:American characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Disney Channel characters]]"
