Senna is a character in Breath of the Wild.


== Biography ==
Senna is a resident of Hateno Village where she greets people for the Kochi Dye Shop. She considers herself be be the hardest worker in greeting, though she admits the weather keeps it from feeling like real work. She can be found in the front of the Shop, where she directs people to her husband, Sayge. She is a taciturn person, telling Link to leave her so she can return to greeting.


== Nomenclature ==
Senna could be named after the Senna plant, a plant used for spices and dyes.
