"\n[[File:X240-oqj.jpg|thumb|250px]] \n'''King Wallace the First''' is the first king of Rodigan and the ancestor of [[King Wallace|King Wallace the Second]] and his son [[Prince Wally]]. He appeared in in the flashback in the Season 1 episode \"[[Royal Pain (episode)|Royal Pain]]\". He is voiced by [[John O'Hurley]].\n\n==Role in episode\u00a0==\nKing Wallace told [[Kim Possible (character)|Kim]] about his ancestor ruling the kingdom with cruel arrogance. In the story, a man begged the king that his whole flock ran away and he needed a few days to pay his taxes, but the king sent him to the tower.
