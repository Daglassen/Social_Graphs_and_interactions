He appears in the episode \"[[Timon on the Range]]\".\n\n==Background==\n===Physical Description===\nCisco Pig is a short and obese warthog with human hands, human ears, a shaven face, mustache that resembles tusks and he wears Old Western clothes. Unlike [[Pumbaa]], he has no warts.\n\n===Personality===\nCisco Pig is likely to be bad since he is a bandit in the Old West. At the end of the episode, he is shown to be good when he saves [[Timon]] and Pumbaa from getting shot by [[Cisco Pig's Gang|his gang]].\n\n==Role in the series==\nCisco Pig and his gang are about to rob a train. When Cisco Pig gets on the track to do so, he keeps missing the train. On his last attempt to rob it, it runs over him, making his gang think he's dead.\n\nLater, when the three bandits are about to murder Timon and Pumbaa, Cisco Pig shows up and stops them.
