Yammo is a character in Breath of the Wild..


== Biography ==
She is a traveling merchant that sells many cooking ingredients.  She travels a large range between Tabantha Bridge Stable and Serenne Stable.  Since she has such a large traveling range, she can be found easier if setting the Sheikah Sensor to search for a Donkey or a Emblazoned Shield, both of which she travels with.
