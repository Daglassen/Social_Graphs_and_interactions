Depending on game progression (which affects the strength of enemies you encounter, as well as the weapons they carry), Blue-Maned Lynels will appear in different places across Hyrule.\n{{Compendium/Extract|Blue-Maned Lynel}}\n{{Term|BotW|Blue-Maned Lynel}} are the blue-colored variant of the red-colored {{Plural|BotW|Lynel|link}}. They have been in {{Term|BotW|Hyrule|link}} since ancient times.<ref>{{Cite|These fearsome monsters have lived in Hyrule since ancient times.|Hyrule Compendium|BotW}}</ref> Like other {{Plural|BotW|Lynel}}, they will often refrain from attacking for a short period of time after spotting {{Term|BotW|Link}}, instead opting to stare down its opponent. Being a higher tier variant from red-colored {{Plural|BotW|Lynel}}, they carry \"Mighty\" set of {{Term|BotW|Lynel Gear|link}}, such as a {{Term|BotW|Mighty Lynel Sword|link}}, {{Term|BotW|Mighty Lynel Shield|link}}, {{Term|BotW|Mighty Lynel Bow|link}}, {{Term|BotW|Mighty Lynel Spear|link}}, and a {{Term|BotW|Mighty Lynel Crusher|link}}.
