Roscoe is a character in Breath of the Wild.


== Biography ==
Roscoe is an Ancient Orb bearing no visual difference to the other orbs in the game. Loone has an affectionate relationship towards Roscoe. Roscoe resides on Puffer Beach in front of the Shoqa Tatone Shrine. Link gains access to Roscoe after completing the "Guardian Slideshow" Shrine Quest, and it is required to access Shoqa Tatone Shrine. Once used to open the Ancient Shrine, Roscoe will disappear.
