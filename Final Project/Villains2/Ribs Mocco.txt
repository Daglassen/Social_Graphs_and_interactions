He was played by actor Robert Beecher. Ribs was a member of the criminal underworld and was present at the meeting held by Big Boy. He was seated next to Influence. Big Boy referred to a past incident in which Ribs had tried to kill him, but Big Boy claimed that it was time to put such incidents behind them.Ribs was present during Tracy's \"raid\" on the Club Ritz, seated at Big Boy's table next to Numbers.\nRibs was later seen being arrested by Tracy while attempting to extort or possibly rob a shop-keeper.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\">\nRibs.jpg\nMovie_-_Ribs_Mocco.jpg\nE862CFAB-3D4A-4410-A58D-C448177A2C66.jpeg\n</gallery>\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Dick Tracy characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Henchmen]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Touchstone characters]]\n[[Category:Mobsters]]\n[[Category:Live-action villains]]\n[[Category:Live-action characters]]"
