Kotta is a character in Breath of the Wild.


== Biography ==
Kotta is a Gerudo soldier who can be found in the barracks in Gerudo Town. If Link speaks to her, she expresses disbelief that the Thunder Helm was stolen, but is interrupted as Liana yells at her for speaking to outsiders. She is then upset that she is in trouble again and blames Link for it. Kotta gets sick while riding a Sand Seal.
If Link speaks to Kotta after completing the Side Quest, "The Search for Barta", she comments about what a mess everything with Barta was and how she is jealous that Barta is somehow popular with men.
