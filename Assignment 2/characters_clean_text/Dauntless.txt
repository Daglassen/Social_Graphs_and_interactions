Dauntless is a character in Breath of the Wild.


== Biography ==
Dauntless is a horse belonging to Nazbi, a Hylian treasure hunter. Together they can be found traveling around Serenne Stable, the Tabantha Bridge Stable, and Snowfield Stable.
Nazbi claims to have become dependent upon Dauntless, being unable to run a few steps on his own without getting winded. Nazbi shows a great deal of affection toward Dauntless, who he describes as his "beloved Dauntless," "dear horse," "noble steed," and "equal partner."
