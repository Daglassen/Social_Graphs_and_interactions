In the past, he was young and strong, but even though he's older now, he still has a lot of fight.<ref>{{Cite|I'm Bohrin of Goron City. I was once young and strong... Now I'm old and wrinkled. But worry not! There's still a lot of fight left in me.|Bohrin|BotW}}</ref> Bohrin works the night shift; during the day he can be found asleep beneath a tarp while the other Gorons work, before waking up at night and working the entire shift alone.<ref>{{Cite|I'm goin' to sleep now. I work the night shift, so if I don't rest now, it'll be tough to get through the night.|Bohrin|BotW}}</ref> By the following morning, he is much too tired and goes straight back to sleep.<ref>{{Cite|I'm so sleepy I can't reply...|Bohrin|BotW}}</ref>\n\nBohrin may be spoken to while he is working. When asked, he'll explain that the ore from the Southern Mine is sold, which {{Term|BotW|Goron City|link}} profits from.<ref>{{Cite|As you can see, this is a mine. We sell the {{Color|BotW Blue|ore}} we pull from the ground. Our home, Goron City, profits from that.|Bohrin|BotW}}</ref> He explains that the Gorons used to mine at the [[Death Mountain Summit]], until the {{Term|BotW|Divine Beast Vah Rudania|link}} appeared and made the area too dangerous.<ref>{{Cite|We used to mine by {{Color|BotW Blue|the summit}}, but with that {{Color|BotW Blue|lizard monster}} launchin' those {{Color|BotW Blue|magma bombs}} at everything...|Bohrin|BotW}}</ref> As a result, the Gorons now work at the Southern Mine.<ref>{{Cite|We just decided to make the most outta diggin' here.|Bohrin|BotW}}</ref> He also gives directions to [[Goron City]], and warns Link that the road ahead is dangerous.<ref>{{Cite|Goron City is that way.
