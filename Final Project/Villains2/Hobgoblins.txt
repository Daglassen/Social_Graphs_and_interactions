They love to terrorize stuff. They even like Leprechaun gold.\n\nA gang of evil ones were seen, but [[Jake Long|Jake]] and [[Haley Long|Haley]] defeated them<ref>[[Family Business (American Dragon: Jake Long)|Family Business]]</ref>.\n\nJake stopped a bunch of Hobgoblins for attacking Leprechauns, unaware they just wanted their gold back. Jake hired them to help stop the Huntsclan to get the one of the [[The 13 Aztec Skulls|Aztec Skulls]] and he repays them a bag full of a lot of Leprechaun gold and two golden motorcycles<ref>[[Fool's Gold]]</ref>.\n\nKrunkers and [[Fu Dog]] were challenging four Hobgoblins in the DMC, but Krunkers just kept hitting Fu a lot of times<ref>[[Nobody's Fu]]</ref>.\n\nThese armored goblins are also the Top 13 Threats to the Magical Community.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\">\nMr. Krunkers and Fu Dog ready to battle with the Goblins.jpg\n</gallery>\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{American Dragon: Jake Long}}\n[[Category:Monsters]]\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:American Dragon: Jake Long characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Reformed characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Legendary creatures]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Disney Channel characters]]"
