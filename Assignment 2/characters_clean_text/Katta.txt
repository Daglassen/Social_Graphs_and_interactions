Katta is a character in Breath of the Wild.


== Biography ==
Katta is a Gerudo soldier who can be found in the palace in Gerudo Town. She guards one of the entrances to Riju's room upstairs. If Link tries to go past her before recovering the Thunder Helm from the Yiga Clan Hideout, she stops him and tells him that he cannot enter without permission. After recovering the Thunder Helm, Katta comments that Link must be special to have permission to enter Riju's bedroom.
