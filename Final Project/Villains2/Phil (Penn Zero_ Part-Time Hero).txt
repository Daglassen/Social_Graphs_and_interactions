He wears a white tank top, dark blue jeans, a brown waist apron with pockets, and black shoes. On his left shoulder, he has a light blue tattoo of a planet with a Saturn-like ring surrounded by six stars and three tiny planets.\n\nIn \"[[At the End of the Worlds]]\", it is revealed that Phil is a cosmic being whose duty is to make sure the Multiverse stays in balance, split in two, with his other half being Phyllis.
