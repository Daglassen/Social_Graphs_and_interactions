"{{Infobox character\n|image = Lloyd Halverson.png\n|films = ''[[White Fang 2: Myth of the White Wolf]]''\n|actor = Matthew Cowles\n|personality = Greedy, violent, cruel, thievish\n|appearance = Slender with a black brim hat and black hunter's clothing\n|occupation = Hunter<br>Trapper<br>Thief<br>Killer\n|alignment = Bad\n|goal = To kill White Fang and rob Henry (failed)\n|home = [[San Francisco]], [[California]]\n|enemies = [[Henry Casey]]<br>[[White Fang (character)|White Fang]]\n|powers = Stealth<br>Gunmanship<br>Traps\n|fate = Limps away in pain after Henry removes the trap from his butt\n}}'''Lloyd Halverson''' is a minor antagonist in [[The Walt Disney Company|Disney]]'s [[1994]] film,\u00a0''[[White Fang 2: Myth of the White Wolf]]''. He is a surly trapper from [[San Francisco]], who wants to rob [[Henry Casey]] and kill [[White Fang (character)|White Fang]].\n\n==Role in the film==\nAfter [[Henry Casey|Henry]] and [[White Fang (character)|White Fang]]'s attempt to hunt a goat, they catch Lloyd trying to sneak in their home. Lloyd introduces himself and claims that he hates his land.\n\nAs Henry and Lloyd talk about why Jack moved back to [[San Francisco]] due to the [[Pre-1922#1906|1906]] earthquake, Lloyd tries to distract Henry and pull out his gun to shoot Henry, only to be stopped by White Fang. As White Fang and Henry confront Lloyd, he flees.\n\nLloyd later returns with a trap to kill White Fang. Lloyd tries to aim at White Fang until a fly buzzes in his ear. White Fang sneaks on him and confronts him as Lloyd falls onto his own trap.\n\nAs he tries to escape from White Fang, he runs into Henry and begs him to get his trap off his buttocks\u00a0or shoot him where he lies. Henry pulls the trap off of him and Lloyd limps away in pain.\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:White Fang characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Hunters]]\n[[Category:Thieves]]\n[[Category:Poachers]]\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Live-action characters]]\n[[Category:Live-action villains]]"
