"{{Infobox character\n|image = 010818.jpg\n|shows = ''[[Hannah Montana]]''<br>''[[The Suite Life on Deck]]''\n|actor = [[Greg Baker]]\n|fullname = Francis Corelli\n|alias = Mr. Corelli\n|personality = Spunky, Funny, Sarcastic, Bossy, Joyful,\n|occupation = Teacher\n|alignment = Neutral/Bad\n}}'''Francis Corelli''' is a character and antagonist from ''[[Hannah Montana]]'' and ''[[The Suite Life on Deck]]''.\n==History==\nHe is a teacher who taught [[Miley Stewart]], [[Oliver]], and [[Lilly Truscott|Lilly]] English, deography, and drama in middle school. He now teaches them history in high school.\n\nCorelli lives with his mother, and is known for his exuberant and loud personality. He favors several students over others, such as celebrity student [[Jake Ryan]].
