Gummi Bears\n|powers = Magic }}\n'''Zorlock''' is an evil wizard from ''[[Adventures of the Gummi Bears]]'' who wants to take over and/or destroy the world.\n\nA foe of the Ancient Gummies, Zorlock accidentally used the Locking Spell to keep him in one spot, which trapped him in his underground cave, way below [[Gummi Glen]], centuries ago.\n\nIn the episode \"[[For Whom the Spell Holds]]\", he turns a harmless rat into a monster named '''Grot''', so that the creature is able to find the ''[[Great Book of Gummi]]'' and deliver it to him in his underground cave, so he could undo the spell which imprisoned him.\n\n==Trivia==\n* Zorlock is also the creator of the [[Doomsday Clock]], as his shadow is seen in a flashback in the episode \"[[A Knight to Remember (Adventures of the Gummi Bears)|A Knight to Remember]]\".\n* He very closely resembles the [[Airiels]] from the episode \"[[Top Gum]]\", which might be an indicator of his origins, as they are revealed to have been sworn enemies of the Ancient Gummies for centuries as well.\n\n==Goofs==\n*Inexplicably, Zorlock is depicted with either four or five fingers on his hands, varying from scene to
