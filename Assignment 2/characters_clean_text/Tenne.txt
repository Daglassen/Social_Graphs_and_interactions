Tenne is a character in Breath of the Wild.


== Biography ==
Tenne is a character in The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild. Tenne is a young female Hylian stable hand who can be found tending the hay troph at the South Akkala Stable in the Akkala Highlands region of Hyrule in the province of Akkala. She is the daughter of the stable's manager Dmitri while the other stable hands Kaifa, Jana, and Gleema are her sisters. She is one of four female stable hands that allows Link to style the mane and change the equipment of the registered horse he currently rides though only if they have bonded with him completely. However like the others she refuses to do this for Epona or the Giant Horse.
