Nekk is a character in Breath of the Wild.


== Biography ==
Nekk runs Brazen Beak, an Armor Shop located in Rito Village. There, he sells the Snowquill Set to Hylian visitors to prepare them for the extremely cold climate north of Rito Village. If Link purchases his entire stock, he will congratulate Link and wonder what else he can possibly offer. He then remembers that Dye Shops exist and recommends that Link purchase another Snowquill Set to dye in different colors.
