He is part of [[Makucha's Army]].\n\n==Background==\n===Personality===\nMuck like [[Makucha]], Fahari is arrogant and unfriendly and is ready to fight any one who come into his territory. However he knows when to back down from a challenge.\n\n==History==\n===[[The Harmattan]]===\nFahari first appears in \"[[The Harmattan]]\" along side Makucha and Jiona when steps into Back Lands on the way to the [[Tree of Life (The Lion Guard)|Tree of Life]]. They start to attack the guard when a huge dust storm hits. Makucha's leap finds the Lion guard in a cave.\n\n=== [[Triumph of the Roar]] ===\nFahari helps Makucha's Army fight the Night Pride and the Lion Guard. He gets defeated because Kion uses the [[Roar of the Elders]] on him and blasts him far far away from the Tree of Life.\n\n==Relationships==\n===Friends===\n====[[Makucha]]====\nFahari is good friends with Makucha.
