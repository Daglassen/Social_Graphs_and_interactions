"{{Infobox_character\n|image = Armand.png\n|name = Armand<br>\u0623\u0631\u0645\u0627\u0646\u062f\n|shows = ''[[Aladdin (TV series)|Aladdin]]''\n|voice = [[Frank Welker]]\n|alias = Squiddy Widdy, Squiddums (by Saleen)\n|personality = Loyal, strong\n|appearance = Pink and yellow spotted octopus\n|occupation = Saleen's pet and minion\n|alignment = Bad, later neutral\n|goal = To protect Saleen and help in her schemes.\n|home = The sea\n|friends = [[Saleen]]\n|enemies = [[Aladdin (character)|Aladdin]], [[Jasmine]], [[Abu]], [[Iago]], [[Genie]], [[Magic Carpet|Carpet]]\n|likes = Being with Saleen, styling hair\n|dislikes = Being pushed aside in favor of Aladdin, ruined hair styles\n|powers = Strength\n|weapons = His tentacles\n|fate = Frees Saleen from a glass bubble.\n}}'''Armand''' is [[Saleen]]'s loyal pet octopus and\u00a0an antagonist from the\u00a0''[[Aladdin (TV series)|Aladdin]]''\u00a0TV series.\n\n==Personality==\nArmand is [[Saleen|Saleen's]] loyal pet and guardian, who will do just about anything to help her. He does, however, have\u00a0a jealous side, which was revealed\u00a0during Saleen's second attempt to steal [[Aladdin (character)|Aladdin]] when she cast him aside resulting in him\u00a0willingly teamed up with [[Genie]] and the others to help break the spell and turning on his mistress.\n\n==Appearances==\nArmand first appeared\u00a0with Saleen in the episode, \"[[Elemental, My Dear Jasmine]],\" he is first seen styling Jasmine's hair, then holding her captive to stop her from getting to the surface during Saleen's first attempt to steal Aladdin and later fighting Genie, Abu,\u00a0Iago, and Carpet. Genie finally overpowers him and\u00a0ties him upside down\u00a0to a fishing post.\n\nIn \"[[Shark Treatment]],\"\u00a0Saleen casts a spell on Aladdin with a mystical starfish turning him into a shark. She completely ignores Armand and he feels jealous and unwanted, so he helps Genie and the others break the spell and even traps his mistress in a glass bubble. Later, when they all\u00a0vanish into the sunset, Armand frees Saleen, who shows her affection to him again and vows that they will get them next time.
