Russ is a character in Breath of the Wild.


== Biography ==
Russ is a member of the Shield Breakers, a shield-surfing gang, consisting of him and his friend Fin. He asks Link to buy from the selection of shields he has, which are used for shield-surfing. If Link purchases a shield, he tells him about the Hylian Shield, which he thinks would be perfect for surfing with. After discussing the shield, he tells Link that there's a rumor of one being in Hyrule Castle.
