Kenyo is a character in Breath of the Wild.


== Biography ==

Kenyo can be found at Tabantha Bridge Stable, near Tabantha Great Bridge. He tells Link of the "Boom-Bam Ghost", and is shocked to find that Link does not know what he is talking about. He goes on to explain that under the Tabantha Great Bridge, there is a ghost that creates loud noises.
