He is the main antagonist of the episode \"[[Leader of the Pack (Goof Troop episode)|Leader of the Pack]]\".\n\n==Background==\n===Personality===\nHe is the 18-year-old leader of a high school gang called the Pharaohs and is shown to dislike [[Max Goof]] and [[P.J.]] seeing them as \"dweebs\" (completely regardless of the fact that they aren't from his school, nor are they even teenagers), and tries to keep them away from coming to Burger Behemoth, by which he calls \"The Pharaoh's Turf\".\n\nDuke is also shown to have romantic feelings for Max's cousin [[Debbie (Goof Troop)|Debbie]] going as far to ditch his own girlfriend for Debbie instead.
