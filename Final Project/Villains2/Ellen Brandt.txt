She was a war veteran turned assassin after her exposure to the virus [[Extremis]].\n\nEllen Brandt was created by Roy Thomas, Gerry Conway and Gray Morrow.\n\n==Role in the film==\n[[Aldrich Killian|Killian]] orders her to retrieve documents who details the death of an individual exposed to [[Extremis]].\nBrandt arrives at the bar and discovers that Tony had already gotten the files, then passed by Homeland Security to \"arrest\" Tony, was only stopped by the Sheriff but she proceeds to kill him and his deputy.\nBrandt and [[Eric Savin|Savin]] chase [[Iron Man|Tony]] to recover the files but fail in their attempt.
