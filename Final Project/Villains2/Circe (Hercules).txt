By the end, thanks to Helen, she turns over a new leaf.\n\n==Trivia==\n*[[Circe (DuckTales)|Another version of Circe]], drawn as a pig, was shown in the series ''[[Ducktales]]''.\n*Circe is the first Disney character to be portrayed by Idina Menzel, who would later portray [[Nancy Tremaine]] in ''[[Enchanted]]'', and [[Elsa]] in ''[[Frozen]]''.\n*In the original Greek mythology, Circe is the daughter of Helios and Perse and is well versed in potions and herbs. Retellings of her story tend to either make her a goddess or simply an enchantress or other supernatural being.
