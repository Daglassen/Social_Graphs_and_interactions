It is also revealed that his name is Ted. The second is a pink pig who is similar to [[Piglet]]. He has a pink shirt. Some people say that his name is Pinky. The third is a tiger who is similar to [[Tigger]]. He has a dark green sweatshirt, a blue cap, and a broken tail with a bandage wrapped around it. It is also revealed that his name is Vacuum Head.\n\nThey make various attempts to get sold instead of [[Rabbit]], who somehow ended up at the toy shop. When Rabbit is eventually bought back by [[Christopher Robin]], the former tells them to keep the tag (which they had ripped after fighting over it) until they become good, and that someone will eventually buy them someday.\n\n== Trivia ==\n* Out of the Toy Villains, Ted is the only member who shares his voice actor (Jim Cummings) with his counterpart Pooh\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery>\nTheToyVillains.jpg\n</gallery>\n\n{{Winnie the Pooh}}\n{{DEFAULTSORT:Toy Villains, The}}\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Winnie the Pooh characters]]\n[[Category:Toys]]\n[[Category:Objects]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Tigers]]\n[[Category:Bears]]\n[[Category:Pigs]]\n[[Category:Villains]]\n\n[[Category:Anthropomorphic characters]]\n[[Category:Character groups]]\n[[Category:Character trios]]\n[[Category:Neutral characters]]\n[[Category:Reformed characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:The Disney Afternoon characters]]"
