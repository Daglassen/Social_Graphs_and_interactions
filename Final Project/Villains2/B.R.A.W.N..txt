"{{Infobox character\n|image = Char 40993.jpg\n|shows = ''[[Mighty Ducks (TV series)|Mighty Ducks]]''\n|voice = [[Frank Welker]]\n|alignment = Bad, later good\n|fullname = Bounty Robotic Assault Weapons Negotiator\n|fate = His body was destroyed but his head remains active and is kept at the Mighty Ducks headquarters.}}'''B.R.A.W.N.'''\u00a0(short for '''Bounty Robotic Assault Weapons Negotiator'''; voiced by [[Frank Welker]])<ref>http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0919798/</ref><ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/Mighty-Ducks-The-Animated-Series/BRAWN/</ref> a large golden robotic peacekeeper who also doubles as a bounty hunter/gaoler of the limbo prison. Serving as jailers whom guarded the incarcerated saurian race, this one had been sent to capture and retrieve [[Lord Dragaunus|Dragaunus]] a.k.a prisoner 476 on Earth. But was reprogrammed by the latter to eliminate the Ducks instead. [[Tanya Vanderflock|Tanya]] was able to re-reprogram him into joining their cause and stopping Dragaunus' assault platform. Almost his entire body was destroyed, but his head still remained.
