They appear in the episode \"[[The McHugger Games]]\".\n\n==Role in the episode==\nThe Robo-Hooligans are sent out to retrieve a pair of McHuggers that [[Randy Cunningham]] stole from [[Hannibal McFist]]. Once [[Pitch Kickham]] tells Randy about the release lever in the back of the McHuggers, Randy frees his hands and takes on the Hooligans. Once Randy takes them out, a second wave appears, but [[Howard Weinerman]] ends up slicing them up with the ID Bot's laser.\n\nThe Hooligans make an appearance in the episode \"[[Escape from Scrap City]]\", implying they were junked after they were defeated.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\">\nMcHuggerGames - 628.jpg\nMcHuggerGames - 649.jpg\nMcHuggerGames - 650.jpg\nMcHuggerGames - 662.jpg\nMcHuggerGames - 675.jpg\nMcHuggerGames - 678.jpg\nMcHuggerGames - 700.jpg\nMcHuggerGames - 726.jpg\nMcHuggerGames - 740.jpg\nMcHuggerGames - 755.jpg\nMcHuggerGames - 763.jpg\nScreen shot 2015-05-05 at 9.27.55 AM.png\nScrap crowd 02.png\n</gallery>\n\n{{Randy Cunningham}}\n[[Category:Randy Cunningham: 9th Grade Ninja characters]]\n[[Category:Character groups]]\n[[Category:Robots]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Those destroyed]]\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Deceased]]\n[[Category:Disney XD characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]"
