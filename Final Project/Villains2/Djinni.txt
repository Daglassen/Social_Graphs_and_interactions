"{{Infobox character\n|image = 454820-vlcsnap 00160 super.jpg\n|name = Djinni\n|shows = ''[[DuckTales]]''<br>''[[DuckTales (2017 series)|DuckTales (2017)]]''<ref>{{cite web|url=https://twitter.com/FrankAngones/status/1240162769640419328|title=Twitter Post|website=Twitter|date=March 18, 2020}}</ref>\n|voice = [[Howard Morris]]\n|alignment = Bad\n|appearance = Turban, vest\n|powers = Granting wishes\n|fate = His lamp ends up getting lost}}The '''Djinni''' is a genie from the ''[[DuckTales]]'' episode \"[[Master of the Djinni]]\". He is not to be confused with [[Gene the Genie]] from ''[[DuckTales the Movie: Treasure of the Lost Lamp]]''. He is shown to be amoral, even getting his master into all sorts of trouble simply to amuse himself, which eventually resulted in both Scrooge and Flintheart calling temporary truce to stop Djinni and seal up his lamp.\n\nThe character's most likely equivalent from the [[DuckTales (2017 series)|2017 reboot series]] is [[Dijon#DuckTales (2017)|Faris D'jinn]], who is both a descendant of a Djinn, and that show's [[Dijon]].
