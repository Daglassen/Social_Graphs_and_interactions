A [[Pluto's Devil|demon version]] of [[Pluto]] first appeared in Mickeys Pal Pluto who appears many times throughout time. [[Chernabog]] is a demon who have a group of [[Chernabog's Minions|minions]] in the movie Fantasia. [[J. Thaddeus Toad|Mr. Toad]] also goes to hell where he sees many demons in [[Mr. Toad's Wild Ride|Mr. Toads Wild Ride.]] [[Hades]] is a demon in the movie [[Hercules]] with his minions [[Pain and Panic]]. In [[Gravity Falls]] a demon named [[Bill Cipher]] who ends up destroying the entirety of the town.
