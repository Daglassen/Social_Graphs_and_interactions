"[[File:The BFG giants 3.png|thumb|250px]]\nThe \"'''Manhugger'''\" is one of the nine man-eating giants that live in Giant Country. He is very tall and slim with short, dark hair and wears a vest and shorts. Surprisingly, the Manhugger is the tallest giant of the gang, surpassing even the Fleshlumpeater in terms of height.\n\nHe along with the [[Fleshlumpeater]], [[Bloodbottler]], [[Meatdripper]], [[Gizzardgulper]], [[Maidmasher]], [[Bonecruncher]], [[Childchewer]], and [[Butcherboy|Butcher Boy]] enjoy snatching and gobbling up little children at night while [[The BFG (character)|The BFG]] delivers dreams to the children.\n[[Category:Live-action characters]]\n[[Category:The BFG characters]]\n[[Category:Monsters]]\n[[Category:Giants]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Live-action villains]]\n[[Category:Immortal]]\n[[Category:Legendary creatures]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]"
