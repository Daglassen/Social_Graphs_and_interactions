"\n[[File:Queen_Liliana.jpg|right|thumb|250px]]'''Queen Liliana'''<ref>http://www.jeffgoode.com/tv/americandragon/characters/liliana.htm</ref> is a character in ''[[American Dragon: Jake Long]]''. She appeared in \"[[Bite Father, Bite Son]]\". She is voiced by [[Tress MacNeille]]<ref>http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0534134/</ref><ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/American-Dragon-Jake-Long/Queen-Lilliana/</ref>.\n\n==Background==\nShe is the mother of one family of [[Strigoi]], vampires which prey on dragons. Shey and three of her sons arrived in New York to kill the American Dragon, but mistook [[Jonathan Long]] for Jake.
