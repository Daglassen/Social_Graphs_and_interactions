She is introduced through a cameo in \"[[The Return of Technor!]]\" and then appears as the antagonist in its partner episode, \"[[A Monumental Crisis!]]\"\n\n==Role in the episode==\nIn \"A Monumental Crisis!\", Teamo's class is on a field trip conducted by Stakas that they greatly dislike. This lack of respect gets her so mad that she makes a plan to steal a lot of historical monuments and take it to her lair. She manages to steal 3 cactus, hands, an igloo, and the Lobster of Liberty, despite Teamo's attempts to stop her and her henchmen, the Dousens.
