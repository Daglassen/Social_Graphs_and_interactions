Depending on the version, he may be a mutant or a normal human empowered by the magical Gem of Cyttorak.\n\nJuggernaut was created by\u00a0[[Stan Lee]]\u00a0and\u00a0[[Jack Kirby]].\n\n==Television Appearances==\n===''[[Ultimate Spider-Man]]''===\nJuggernaut caused rampage around New York and encountered [[Spider-Man]] for several days, but with his sheer strength was to overcome Spider-Man's webbings and escape. Juggernaut was in hiding and sat in a train where he encountered Spider-Man who was trying to stop [[Awesome Android]] . Juggernaut ambushed Spider-Man and fought against him and the other trainees. He was eventually defeated when his clothing was eaten by Awesome Android\u00a0and was knocked unconscious by Spider-Man.\n\n==Trivia==\n*He, [[Wolverine]], [[Deadpool]],  [[Arcade]], and [[Sabretooth ]] are the only [[X-Men (team)|X-Men]] characters to appear in any of the Disney XD/Marvel animated shows.\n*The character was portrayed by Vinnie Jones in X Men: The Last Stand and later in voice and motion capture by Ryan Reynolds in Deadpool 2 who also portrays the title character.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\">\njuggernaut-DWA.jpg\nJuggernaut POP.jpg\n</gallery>\n\n{{Marvel Animated Universe}}\n\n\n\n{{USMWiki}}\n[[Category:Marvel Comics characters]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Giants]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Ultimate Spider-Man characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Marvel Disk Wars: The Avengers characters]]\n[[Category:Mutants]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Magic Users]]\n[[Category:Characters in video games]]\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Acquired characters]]\n[[Category:X-Men Cinematic Universe]]\n[[Category:Disney XD characters]]"
