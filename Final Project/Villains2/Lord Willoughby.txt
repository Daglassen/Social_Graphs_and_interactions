"{{Infobox character\n|image = Screen Shot 2015-01-10 at 8.56.08 am.png\n|shows = ''[[Adventures of the Gummi Bears]]''\n|voice = [[Will Ryan]]\n|home = Willoughby Manor\n|likes = Scheming\n|dislikes = [[Sir Gawain]] telling everyone stories\n|status = Gets taken away when everyone thinks that he's crazy\n}}\n'''Lord Willoughby''' is the main antagonist of the ''[[Adventures of the Gummi Bears]]\" episode \"[[He Who Laughs Last]]\".\n\n==History==\nHe is very bad-tempered and arrogant with a dislike of [[Sir Gawain]] and his stories involving [[Gummi Bears]].\n\nHe first attempts to have Gawain declared a nut before [[King Gregor]], who dismisses the accusation as harmless tall tales, and that he is not going to institutionalize a loyal knight of his, on such flimsy charges.\n\nWilloughby then plays another move; by challenging Sir Gawain to bet his entire fortune against his own, that Gummi Bears do exist.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery widths=200 spacing=small position=center captionalign=center>\nAdventuresOfTheGummiBears-HeWhoLaughsLast-SunniBoo.png|Sunni scaring Lord Willoughby\n</gallery>\n\n==Trivia==\n*Lord Willoughby is one of a relatively small group of humans who find out about the existence of Gummi Bears during the course of the series. Other humans that become explicitly aware of the bears, are [[Cavin]], [[Princess Calla]], [[Duke Igthorn]], [[Lady Bane]], [[Sir Gawain]], [[Princess Marie]], [[Angelo Davini]], [[Flint Shrubwood]], [[The Hunter|the Hunter]], [[Rotocelli]], [[Doctor Dexter]], [[Marzipan]], [[Prince Yen Moon]] and his subjects, Carney's assistant from the episode \"[[Never Give a Gummi an Even Break]]\", and the boy who turned himself into a Giant, from the episode \"[[When You Wish Upon a Stone]]\".\n\n{{Adventures of the Gummi Bears}}\n{{DEFAULTSORT:Willoughby, Lord}}\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Adventures of the Gummi Bears characters]]\n[[Category:Royalty]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:The Disney Afternoon characters]]\n[[Category:European characters]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Males]]"
