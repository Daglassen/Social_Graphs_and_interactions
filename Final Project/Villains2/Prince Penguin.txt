He hates the cold, but believes he will melt if not kept cold due to some legends of his people.\n\n===Physical appearance===\nHe is a large penguin dressed in elaborate royal clothes and an odd shaped crown. He has a moustache on his beak and wears a monocle. He wears several rings.
