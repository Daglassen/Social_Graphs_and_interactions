"{{Infobox_character\n|image = JIMMY.jpg\n|name = Jimmy Bartin\n|shows = ''[[Good Luck Charlie]]''\n|actor = Ryan Heinke\n|birthday = October 1, 1992\n|occupation = Band member/bass player\n|alignment = Bad\n|alliance = PJ and the Vibe (formerly)\n|home = Denver, Colorado\n|family = Logan (possibly)\n|friends = [[PJ Duncan]], [[Emmett Heglin]]\n|enemies = [[Teddy Duncan]]}}'''Jimmy Bartin''' is a character and antagonist in ''[[Good Luck Charlie]]''.\n\n==History==\nHe was a bass player in PJ's band. He only appeared in Teddy Rebounds. Jimmy is the only guy that PJ and Emmett accepted when they were auditioning for a bass player. He play's PJ and Emmett's jingle called Monkeys shouldn't fly. Charlie sneezed on him so he couldn't play with him in their first gig, instead [[Bob Duncan|Bob]] had to fill in for him. Although Jimmy was sick, PJ, Bob & Emmett still performed in his backyard. Jimmy had missed the gig that was in his own backyard.
