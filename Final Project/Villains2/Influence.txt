"{{Infobox character\n|name = Influence\n|image = Influence.png\n|films = ''[[Dick Tracy]]''\n|actor = Henry Silva\n|alignment = Bad\n|home = [[Chicago]]\n|weapons = Tommy gun\n|fate = Gets shot by Dick Tracy's Tommy gun\n|quote = \"Hey, when do we kill Dick Tracy?\"}}'''Influence''' is a character in ''[[Dick Tracy]]''. He was portrayed by Henry Silva (the voice of Bane in Batman the Animated Series). He worked closely with Pruneface. He, like his associates, was killed in a firefight with Tracy and the police outside the Club Ritz.\n\nHis only line in the movie was in the conference with Big Boy- \"Hey, when do we kill Dick Tracy?\".
