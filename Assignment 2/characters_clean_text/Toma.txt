Toma is a character in Breath of the Wild.


== Biography ==
Toma is a fashion-oriented traveler who can be found traversing the road between Fort Hateno and Hateno Village on horseback. She speaks about using clothing as a means of expression, often valuing form over function. She advises Link to wear fashionable outfits to combat the unfortunate events plaguing Hyrule. She also recommends that Link carries multiple sets of clothing in order to adapt to situations.
