[[Yondu Udonta]] a leader within the group and raised [[Star-Lord|Peter Quill]] at youth as one of the ravagers.\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[Guardians of the Galaxy (film)|Guardians of the Galaxy]]''===\nIn 1988, Peter Quill's father sends the Ravagers to Earth to bring his son with him but instead their leader, Yondu decides to keep and raise as a ravager.\nTwenty six years later, the Ravagers going to Morag and there they learn that Quill betrayed them, then Yondu offers a bounty on his head.\n\nBeing in [[Knowhere]], they find and save Quill and Gamora. Yondu and Peter makes a deal, they help them defeat Ronan and he gives them the [[Orb]].\nAfter the Battle of Xandar, Yondu forces Peter to hand over the Orb, while the ravagers leaving Xandar, Yondu says he's glad not to have been Peter with his father. Then, while traveling, Yondu discovers that Peter didn't give him the true Orb but he just smiles.\n\n====''[[What If...?|What if...?]]''====\nIn one universe T'Challa was abducted by mistake when Yondu went to get Ego's child. Thanks to T'Challa's influence, Yondu's faction became [[Robin Hood (character)|Robin Hood]] inspired heroes, earning fame and praise across the cosmos; with the Kree Empire being their main nemesis and Ronan still out there on his own. They even prvented [[Drax the Destroyer]] from losing his family. Thanks to T'Challa using diplomacy, Thanos was talked out of his plans and joined with Nebula; T'Challa tries to get them into counseling.
