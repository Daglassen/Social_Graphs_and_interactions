At the northern edge of {{Term|BotW|Central Hyrule}} lies {{Term|BotW|Hyrule Castle (Breath of the Wild)|link|display= Hyrule Castle}}, which holds {{Term|BotW|Calamity Ganon|link}}.\n\nIt is known, during the time of {{Term|BotW|Calamity Ganon}}, as one of the most dangerous {{Plural|BotW|Region}} in {{Term|BotW|Hyrule|link}}, due to vast amounts of {{Term|BotW|Guardian|link|display= Guardians}} being present there and myriad monsters, such as {{Plural|BotW|Bokoblin|link}}.\n\n====Great Hyrule Forest====\n{{Main|Great Hyrule Forest}}\nThe Great Hyrule Forest is a large wooded area that covers the vast majority of northern Central Hyrule,<ref>{{Cite Book|quote='''{{Big|Central Hyrule}}''' |book= CaC |publisher= Dark Horse Books |page= 221}}</ref> consisting of smaller woods that together make up the entire area. The [[Lost Woods]] is the largest of all its forests, which surrounds the [[Korok Forest]] in its core, and is itself surrounded by a deep gorge known as [[Lake Mekar]]. The Great Hyrule Forest further extends north toward the {{Term|BotW|Eldin Mountains|link}}, ending at the isolated [[Thyphlo Ruins]].\n\n====Great Plateau====\n{{Main|Great Plateau}}\n\nThe {{Term|BotW|Great Plateau}}, in the southwest part of {{Term|BotW|Central Hyrule}}, is the subregion of said area where {{Term|BotW|Link}} begins his quest and leaves to journey the rest of {{Term|BotW|Hyrule}}.\n\n====Hyrule Field====\n{{Main|Hyrule Field}}\n\n{{Term|BotW|Hyrule Field}} is the subregion making up the majority of {{Term|BotW|Central Hyrule}}, and a good amount of {{Term|BotW|Hyrule}} in general. It contains many monsters and {{Plural|BotW|Guardian|link}}, as well as {{Term|BotW|Hyrule Castle (Breath of the Wild)|link|display= Hyrule Castle}}.\n\n====Hyrule Ridge====\n{{Main|Hyrule Ridge}}\n\n{{Term|BotW|Hyrule Ridge}}, in the northwest part of {{Term|BotW|Central Hyrule|link}}, is a subregion with a rockier terrain and higher elevated areas, with the the major part of it consisting of the {{Term|BotW|Thundra Plateau|link}}.\n\n==Other Appearances==\n==={{HWAoC|-}}===\n{{Noncanon|Start}}\n{{Stub|HWAoC}}\n{{Noncanon|End}}\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery>\nBotW CaC Central Hyrule Map.png|Region boundaries as seen in {{CaC}}\n</gallery>\n\n{{Ref}}\n\n{{Categories\n |Central Hyrule, Regions\n |locations= BotW, HWAoC\n}}"
