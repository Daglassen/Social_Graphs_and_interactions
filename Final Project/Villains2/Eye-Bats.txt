Eye-Bats from Gravity Falls have black wings and are as large as regular bats, while Eye-Bats from the Nightmare Realm have red wings and are larger than a human. Aside from their wings and size difference, both have a yellow eyeball.\n\n===Abilities===\nNormal Eye-Bats mostly just fly around and avoid humans. Eye-Bats from the Nightmare Realm can project a red light that freezes people into stone, and take them away.\n\n==Role in the series==\nThe Eye-Bats first appeared during a flashback in \"[[A Tale of Two Stans]]\" as [[Ford Pines|Ford]] captures at least a few and writes about them in his\u00a0[[Journal 1|Journal]].\u00a0\n\nThey are revealed to be working for\u00a0[[Bill Cipher]]\u00a0and [[Henchmaniacs|his demon pals]]\u00a0in \"[[Weirdmageddon]]\", when during [[Weirdmageddon (event)|the event]], a number of Eye-Bats turned people into stone then capture and take them to the\u00a0Fearamid, the whole downtown of\u00a0Gravity Falls\u00a0was covered with Eye-Bats flying around the openings surveying the areas for civilians. During the final battle, at least a few Eye-Bats are destroyed by [[Shacktron|the Shacktron robot]].\n\nSometime after, the remaining Eye-Bats are chased out away by [[Sprott]].\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\">\nS2e12 eye bats page.jpg\nS2e12 eye bats in jar.jpg\nS2e18 eye bat close up 2.jpg\nS2e18 eye bat close up.jpg\nS2e20 eye-bat closer.png\nS2e20 eye bats around the Shacktron.png\nS2e20 eye bat falling.png\nS2e20 eye bat turned to stone.png\nS2e20_Wendy_on_the_eye_bat.png|Wendy on an Eye-Bat.\nS2e20_Wendy_still_riding_the_eye_bat.png\nS2e18_a_look_at_gravity_falls.jpg|A number of Eye-Bats patrolling Gravity Falls.\nS2e20_eye_bat_zapped.png|An Eye-Bat get blasted by Rumble.\ntumblr_o1ogpbCfMh1ubw913o1_1280.jpg\nS2e20_Gobblewonker_eats_eye_bat.png\nS2e19_flying_off.png\nS2e20_dino_vs_eye_bat.png\nGF_Bill's_minions.png\nS2e20_stone_raccoon.png|An Eye-Bat petrifying a raccoon at the Mystery Shack.\nS2e18_party_of_the_demons.jpg\nS2e19_Party.png\nS2e20_eye_bats_fleeing.png\nS2e20_Sprott_shooing_eye_bats.png|Eye-Bats' defeat.\n</gallery>\n\n{{Gravity Falls}}\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Gravity Falls characters]]\n[[Category:Bats]]\n[[Category:Characters who fly]]\n[[Category:Those destroyed]]\n[[Category:Monsters]]\n[[Category:Mystical animals]]\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Neutral characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Silent characters]]\n[[Category:Gravity Falls]]"
