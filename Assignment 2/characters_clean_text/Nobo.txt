Nobo is a character in Breath of the Wild.


== Biography ==
Nobo is a female Hylian from The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild. She is located at the East Akkala Stable in the Akkala Highlands region of Hyrule and provides Link with the Shrine quest "The Spring of Power". The horse bound male Hylian traveler Pitar mention becoming curious about her after meeting her and notes she had some good info
