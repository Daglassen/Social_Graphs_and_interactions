"The '''\"Meatdripper\"''' is one of the nine child-eating giants that live in giant country. Meatdripper is best friends with the [[Childchewer]] and appears to be the most well-groomed of the man-eating giants. He is most distinguished by red Mohawk hair.\n\nHe, along with the [[Fleshlumpeater]], [[Bloodbottler]], [[Butcherboy|Butcher Boy]], [[Gizzardgulper]], [[Maidmasher]], [[Manhugger]], [[Childchewer]], and [[Bonecruncher]], enjoy snatching and gobbling up little children at night while [[The BFG (character)|The BFG]] delivers dreams to the children.\n[[Category:The BFG characters]]\n[[Category:Giants]]\n[[Category:Monsters]]\n[[Category:Creatures]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Legendary creatures]]\n[[Category:Immortal]]\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Live-action villains]]"
