She is the leader of a group of skinks that work for [[Ushari]].\n\n==Background==\n===Personality===\nShupavu is sly and sneaky with a somewhat nasty streak. Although prideful, she is not above helping others, especially if it will benefit a cause she believes in.\n\n===Physical appearance===\nShupavu is slender and long-bodied, with skin of a deep red hue. She has lighter red patches on her back, neck and face, contrasting with her burgundy underbelly and eyespots. Shupavu's eyes are fringed with a yellow mark and framed by dark eyelashes.\n\n==Role in the series==\n===[[The Rise of Scar|''The Lion Guard: The Rise of Scar'']]===\nDuring the musical sequence \"[[Fujo]]\", Shupavu and [[Njano]] watched\u00a0the [[Lion Guard]]\u00a0performing their duties before they slithered away.\n\nLater, Shupavu and her skinks are summoned by\u00a0Ushari. He requests that one of the skinks follow\u00a0[[Rafiki]]\u00a0and\u00a0[[Makini]]\u00a0to the\u00a0[[Lair of the Lion Guard]]\u00a0in order to find out of there is a way to speak to the Evil\u00a0Lions of the Past. Shupavu agrees and tasks her stealthiest skink,\u00a0Nyeusi, with the job.\n\nBoth Shupavu and Ushari accompany Nyeusi to the entrance of the Lair, and Ushari thanks the skinks for their help, asserting that he can finish the task. Shupavu departs with Nyeusi, eager to get way from\u00a0[[Pride Rock]].\n\n=== ''The Lion Guard'' ===\n====''[[Let Sleeping Crocs Lie]]''====\nWhen\u00a0[[Makuu]]\u00a0leaves with\u00a0the Lion Guard\u00a0to speak to\u00a0[[Simba]]\u00a0about new sleeping arrangements,\u00a0[[Kiburi]]\u00a0seizes control of [[Makuu's Float|the float]], inspiring them to join him in taking over the water holes in the\u00a0[[Pride Lands]]. From afar, Shupavu watches in fascination and resolves to speak with\u00a0Ushari\u00a0and\u00a0[[Scar]]\u00a0about the proceedings.\n\nAt\u00a0[[Janja's Den]], Shupavu tells Scar about Kiburi, and he orders her and her skinks to keep an eye on the goings on. Later, Shupavu informs Scar that Kiburi has challenged Makuu to a\u00a0Mashindano.\n\n====''[[Swept Away]]''====\nWhen [[Beshte]] is alone in the [[Outlands|Outlands,]] Shupavu helps trap him.\n\n====''[[The Little Guy]]''====\nA gecko named [[Hodari]] wants to be a crocodile so Shupavu introduces him to [[Kiburi's Float]].\n\n====''[[The Scorpion's Sting]]''====\nShupavu helps the rest of [[Army of Scar|Scar's army]] trap the Lion Guard. She soon gets blasted away by Kion's [[Roar of the Elders]].\n\n====''[[Undercover Kinyonga]]''====\nShupavu's group catches a chameleon spying on Scar.\n\n====''[[The Hyena Resistance]]''====\nShupavu finds out that [[Jasiri]] and [[Jasiri's Clan|her clan]] have been working with the Lion Guard to stop Scar.\n\n====''[[The Underground Adventure]]''====\nShupavu reports to Scar that Kion, [[Kiara]], [[Bunga]], [[Tiifu]] and [[Zuri]] are in the Mud Pots.\n\n====''[[Pride Landers Unite!]]''====\nShupavu overhears that Kion has gathered [[Pride Animals]] to help fight Scar.\n\n====''[[The Queen's Visit]]''====\nShupavu tells Scar about Queen [[Dhahabu]].\n\n====''[[The Fall of Mizimu Grove]]''====\nShupavu tells Scar about the show in Mizimu Grove.\n\n====''[[Battle for the Pride Lands]]''====\nShupavu and Njano overhear Kion's plan to invade the Outlands and go to tell Scar, who tells her to help Janja find the Lion Guard's lair and bring the other Outland leaders for the final attack.
