He is the younger brother to [[Morpheus (Hercules)|Morpheus]] who was jealous of his brother's job as the God of Sleep and attempted to replace him until he reformed became the God of Dreams.\n\n==Powers and Abilities==\nAs a god, Phantasos possesses the natural powers & abilities of an Olympian gods, in addition to his own power of dream manipulation.\n\n==Role in the [[Hercules (TV series)|series]]==\nIn \"[[Hercules and the Long Nightmare]]\", Phantasos became angered and jealous when his brother Morpheus was chosen to be the God of Sleep instead of him. Wanting the job for himself, Phantasos created his quilt of dreams to give mortals nightmares to sabotaging his brother, knowing Morpheus would be blamed and the job would be passed down to him.\n\n{{Stubsection}}\n\n==Trivia==\n*In mythology, Phanatasos is in truth a Oneiroi, one of the gods of dreams. And represents inanimate objects.
