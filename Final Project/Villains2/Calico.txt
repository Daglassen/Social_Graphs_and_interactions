"{{Infobox character\n|image = Purpleguy.JPG\n|films = ''[[Muppet Treasure Island]]<br>[[The Muppets' Wizard of Oz]]<br>[[Muppets Most Wanted]]''\n|games = ''[[Muppet Treasure Island (video game)|Muppet Treasure Island]]''\n|actor = [[Jerry Nelson]]\n|alignment = Neutral\n|friends = [[Animal (Muppet)|Animal]], [[Fozzie Bear]], [[Walter]]\n|shows = ''[[The Muppets (TV series)|The Muppets]]''}}'''Calico''' is a pinkish monocle-wearing character who appears in the film,\u00a0''[[Muppet Treasure Island]]'' as a pirate. He also appears in the video game, which is where his name comes from.\n\n==History==\nHe first appears in the \"[[Sailing for Adventure]]\" number singing a solo in the first verse. He also appears in the \"[[Cabin Fever]]\" number, where he and an unnamed character take a verse. One of the pirates pulls a knife on him during \"[[A Professional Pirate]]\". Yet, he is still alive in the next scene. He also appears in the Muppet Treasure Island Sing Along performed by another puppeteer.\n\nHe reappears in ''[[The Muppets' Wizard of Oz]]'' as one of the Flying Monkeys, along with several of his fellow pirates.\n\nHe also appears in\u00a0''[[Muppets Most Wanted]]''\u00a0as a\u00a0[[Gulag 38B]]\u00a0inmate, and first appears in the song\u00a0\"[[Working in the Coal Mine]]\", assisting\u00a0[[Animal (Muppet)|Animal]],\u00a0[[Fozzie Bear|Fozzie]], and\u00a0[[Walter]]\u00a0to dig a tunnel for\u00a0[[Kermit the Frog|Kermit]].\n\n==Trivia==\n*He bears a striking resemblance to [[w:c:muppets:Count von Count|Count von Count]]\u00a0from the children's puppet show ''[[wikipedia:Sesame Street|Sesame Street]]''.\n\n{{MuppetsWiki}}\n{{The Muppets}}\n[[Category:The Muppets characters]]\n[[Category:Muppet Treasure Island characters]]\n[[Category:Singing characters]]\n[[Category:Pirates]]\n[[Category:Henchmen]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Muppets Most Wanted characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:The Muppets' Wizard of Oz characters]]\n[[Category:Neutral characters]]\n[[Category:Acquired characters]]\n[[Category:Live-action characters]]"
