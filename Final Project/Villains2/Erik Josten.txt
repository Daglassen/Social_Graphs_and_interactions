He was genetically modified with ionic rays, which gave him the ability to grow as tall as a skyscraper, superhuman strength, and invulnerability. He decided to use his powers as the supervillain '''Goliath''', joining the Masters of Evil to defeat the Avengers.\nHe later resurfaces as the superhero '''Atlas''', helping the Avengers defeat the [[Growing Man]].\n\nErik Josten was created by\u00a0[[Stan Lee]],\u00a0[[Jack Kirby]],\u00a0and Don Heck.\n\n==Powers & Abilities==\n* '''Superhuman Strength:'''\u00a0His natural super strength is beyond human limits. It rivals the strength of a calm & collected Hulk. His strength enhances as he grows in size & mass.\u00a0\n* '''Size & Mass Enhancement:'''\u00a0His physiology has the ability to enhance its mass & physical size upon will. This also enhances his strength & durability to greater limits.\n** '''Superhuman Durability:'''\u00a0As his physiology increases, so does his natural resilience to damage.\u00a0He can withstand powerful strikes from beings with great superhuman strength such as Hulk, and strong hits from Thor's hammer Mjolnir with minor discomfort.\n* '''Hand-to-Hand Combat:'''\u00a0He displays great combat skills & athletic prowess in combat.
