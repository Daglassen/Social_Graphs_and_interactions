"{{Infobox character\n|image = Sherri.png\n|name = Sherri\n|shows = ''[[I Didn't Do It]]''\n|actress = [[Peyton List]]\n|alignment = Bad\n}}\n'''Sherri''' is a minor antagonist in ''[[I Didn't Do It]]'' and appears in the episode \"Dance Fever\". She is portrayed by [[Peyton List]].\n\n==Biography==\n===Season One===\nSherri tries to ruin [[Lindy Watson|Lindy]]'s perfect attendance record. She is supposedly the nicest girl in school, but she is really obsessed with being the only one who has perfect attendance, to the point of potential insanity and bordering on meglomania. She has ruined other students' perfect attendance.\n\n==Appearance==\nSherri has blonde hair and blue eyes.\n\n==Trivia==\n*She tried to ruin Lindy's perfect attendance record and would even hurt Lindy to achieve this.\n*She greatly dislikes Lindy.\n{{I Didn't Do It}}\n{{CharacterStub}}\n[[Category:I Didn't Do It characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Live-action characters]]\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Live-action villains]]\n[[Category:Teenagers]]\n[[Category:American characters]]\n[[Category:Anti-heroes]]\n[[Category:Students]]\n[[Category:Disney Channel characters]]"
