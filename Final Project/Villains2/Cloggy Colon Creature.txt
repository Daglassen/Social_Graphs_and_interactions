"{{Infobox character\n|image = Cloggy Colon Creature.png\n|shows = ''[[Super Robot Monkey Team Hyperforce Go!]]''\n|voice = [[Kevin Michael Richardson]]\n|alignment = Bad\n}}\n'''Cloggy Colon Creature''' is the head of Wonder Fun Meat World and an antagonist from ''[[Super Robot Monkey Team Hyperforce Go!]]''. He was voiced by [[Kevin Michael Richardson]].\n\n==Role in the series==\nThe character first takes the appearance of a box of food like you get at a restaurant, and has three friends, a cola cup, a box of fries, and a cone of meat on a stick, which all three mutate into strange creatures when hit with a vegetable drink. Vegetables is the only weakness of the Cloggy colon creature and it burns his skin when he is touched by it.
