He succeeded in capturing [[Dipper Pines|Dipper]] and [[Ford Pines|Ford]], with some assistance from Hot Elf, an ogre and the griffin. He revealed that wanted to eat their brains so that he could gain their intelligence. He later challenges [[Mabel Pines|Mabel]] and [[Stan Pines|Stan]] to a board game of Dungeons, Dungeons, and More Dungeons. During the game, the Probabilitor conjures different spells and summons various creatures that the humans fight against, including the Ogre-nado and the Impossibeast.
