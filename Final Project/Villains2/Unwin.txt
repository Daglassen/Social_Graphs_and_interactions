"{{Infobox character\n|name = Unwin\n|image = Gummibears_beginning02.jpg\n|shows = ''[[Adventures of the Gummi Bears]]''\n|voice = [[Will Ryan]]\n|occupation = Squire\n|home = [[Dunwyn Castle]] \n|likes = Picking on [[Cavin]], [[Princess Calla]], practice sword fighting skills\n|dislikes = [[Ogres]], being beaten by Cavin and Princess Calla, Cavin\u2019s belief in Gummi Bears\n}}\n'''Unwin''' is a character who appears in the [[Disney Afternoon]] series ''[[Adventures of the Gummi Bears]]''.\n\nUnwin is a squire at [[King Gregor]]'s court at [[Dunwyn Castle]], under [[Sir Tuxford]]. He is portrayed as a bully toward [[Cavin]], but he is shown to be cowardly whenever he runs away in fear of the [[Ogres]], or during a trial in which he has to face the [[Mysterious Knight]]. In contrast to Cavin, he is also known to be a non-believer in [[Gummi Bears]].\n\n==Appearances==\nHe appears in only nine episodes, including the pilot episode \"[[A New Beginning]]\", which was his debut appearance.
