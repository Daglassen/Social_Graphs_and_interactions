"{{Group Infobox\n|image = Rocket-trooper.jpg\n|shows = ''[[Star Wars Rebels]]''\n|games = ''[[Star Wars: Commander]]<br>[[Star Wars Battlefront]]<br>[[Star Wars Battlefront II]]''\n|alias = Imperial Jump Troopers<br>Rocket Troopers\n|affiliation = [[Galactic Empire]]\n|alignment = Bad\n|goal = To serve the Empire}}\n\n'''Jumptroopers''' (also known as\u00a0'''Imperial Jump Troopers'''\u00a0or\u00a0'''rocket troopers''') were specialized\u00a0stormtroopers\u00a0utilized by the\u00a0[[Galactic Empire]] in the ''[[Star Wars]]'' Universe. Prior to and during the\u00a0Galactic Civil War, the jumptroopers were equipped with\u00a0jump packs\u00a0or\u00a0jetpacks\u00a0that allowed them temporary flight.\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[Star Wars Rebels]]''===\nJumptroopers appeared in the episode\u00a0\"[[Ghosts of Geonosis]]\", a contingent of Jumptroopers were attached to\u00a0Captain\u00a0Brunson's\u00a0Imperial light cruiser. These Jumptroopers attempted to board the\u00a0Lothal rebels' ship\u00a0''[[Ghost (starship)|Ghost]]''\u00a0but were repelled by the rebels,\u00a0[[Rex (Star Wars)|Rex]], and\u00a0[[Saw Gerrera]]. After the entire unit was killed, Brunson ordered her crew to bury the\u00a0''Ghost''\u00a0by collapsing an air shaft on top of them.\n\nIn \"[[Zero Hour]]\", Several Jumptroopers were also aboard one of the Interdictor Cruisers that joined the Seventh Fleet for the Battle of Atollon, and engaged [[Ezra Bridger]] and the [[Mandalorian]] Warriors of Clan Wren when they attacked the ship.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Jumptroopers first appeared in the\u00a0[[1993]]\u00a0video game\u00a0''Super Star Wars: The Empire Strikes Back''\u00a0as\u00a0'''airtroopers'''.\u00a0The\u00a0''Star Wars Expaned Universe''\u00a0later canonized the concept in their continuity. On\u00a0[[April 25]],\u00a0[[2014]],\u00a0the\u00a0''Star Wars''\u00a0Expanded Universe was declared non-canon,\u00a0and rebranded as\u00a0Legends.\u00a0Imperial Jump Troopers were later reintroduced into canon in the\u00a02014\u00a0mobile game\u00a0''[[Star Wars: Commander]]'',\u00a0appearing visually similar to the\u00a0Imperial seen in the\u00a0[[2008]]\u00a0Legends video game\u00a0''Star Wars: The Force Unleashed''.\n*In the\u00a0[[2015]]\u00a0video game\u00a0''[[Star Wars Battlefront]]'', jumptroopers wore generic stormtrooper armor with matching jump packs.\n\n== Gallery ==\n<gallery widths=\"150\" position=\"center\" spacing=\"small\">\nSWBF2 rocket trooper.png\nGhosts of Geonosis concept 3.jpeg|Concept art\nGhosts of Geonosis 31.jpeg\nGhosts of Geonosis 24.jpeg\nGhosts of Geonosis 14.jpg\nStar-wars-jumptrooper-sixth-scale-hot-toys-silo-.png\nLego-jumptrooper.jpg|[[LEGO]] Minifigure \n</gallery>\n\n==See also==\n*[[Stormtroopers]]\n*[[First Order Stormtroopers]]\n*[[Death Troopers]]\n*[[Scout Troopers]]\n\n==External links==\n*{{Interwiki|https://vignette3.wikia.nocookie.net/starwars/images/6/64/Favicon.ico|starwars|Jumptrooper|Wookieepedia}}\n\n{{Star Wars}}\n\n[[Category:Galactic Empire characters]]\n[[Category:Star Wars Rebels characters]]\n[[Category:Soldiers]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Star Wars villains]]\n[[Category:Characters in video games]]"
