"{{Species Infobox\n|image = Caledonian Boar.png\n|films=''[[Hercules (film)|Hercules]]''|shows=''[[Hercules (TV series)|Hercules]]''|inspiration=[https://www.greekmythology.com/Myths/Monsters/Calydonian_Boar/calydonian_boar.html Claedorian Boar]|alias=Giant Boar<br> Erymanthian Boar (often mistaken for)|behaviour=Hostile|abilities=Strength|status=Deceased}}The '''Caledonian Boar''' is a minor antagonist in both the [[1997]] ''[[Hercules (film)|Hercules ]]'' film and the ''[[Hercules (TV series)|Hercules]]'' TV series.\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[Hercules (film)|Hercules]]''===\nIn the Hercules movie, the Caledonian Boar was the first beast that [[Hercules (character)|Hercules]] faced during the [[Zero to Hero]] montage. He is first seen as one of [[Hades]]'s pawn pieces, and then the scene switches to the giant boar about to charge Hercules. Hercules quickly grabs a bow and arrow, firing it at the beast. The camera zooms in on the boar's shocked face as it makes collision. The scene then changes to a paining of the boar on a vase where his corpse is being served on a plate by Hercules to [[Philoctetes]].\n\n===[[Hercules (TV series)|''Hercules'' TV Series]]===\nIn the TV series, a Caledonian Boar makes an appearance in the episode ''[[Hercules and the Caledonian Boar]]''. In this episode, Hercules and Philoctetes go into the woods to hunt down a Caledonian Boar. They eventually find one, but Hercules finds himself unwilling kill it. The boar instead chases and embraces them in front of others who were hunting in the woods during the same time they were. Later in the episode, Philoctetes is turned into a boar by [[Artemis]] for hunting in her woods. \n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=center>\nCalydonian boar pawn.png|The Caledonian boar's pawn piece from the film. \nCalydonian boar defeated.png|The deceased Caledonian boar painted on a vase. \nCaledonian Boar TV series Hercules.png|A Caledonian boar from the TV series. \n</gallery>\n\n==Trivia==\n*The Caledonian boar is often mistaken by fans for another kind of mythological giant boar known as the Erymanthian Boar.
