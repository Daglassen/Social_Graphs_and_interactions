Golden Lizalfos are enemies in Breath of the Wild. They are exclusive to the Master Mode included in The Master Trials DLC Pack.


== Characteristics ==

In Master Mode, golden enemies like Golden Lizalfos appear as the strongest variety of each enemy. Golden Lizalfos have a high amount of health at 1296. They are said to be Silver Lizalfos who were struck by lightning and mysteriously transformed. Due to this, they are resilient to electric attacks. They equip strong Weapons that have various bonuses. When defeated, they may drop Lizalfos Horns, Lizalfos Tails, Lizalfos Talons, Amber, Opal, Topaz, Rubies, Sapphires, and Diamonds.
