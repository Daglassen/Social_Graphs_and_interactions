He is a former employee of [[Stark Industries]] who feels spurned by [[Iron Man|Tony Stark]]\u00a0and decided to turn into the villain '''Fixer'''. He uses his brilliant scientific mind to create machines and weapons that aid him in his supervillain career, including his very own suit of power armor. As Fixer, he is a member of the [[Masters of Evil]].\nHe later resurfaces as the superhero\u00a0'''Techno''', helping the Avengers defeat\u00a0[[Growing Man]].\n\nPaul Ebersol was created by\u00a0[[Stan Lee]]\u00a0and\u00a0[[Jack Kirby]].\n\n== Appearance ==\nAs Fixer, he first appears in the episode \"Under Siege\" as a member of the\u00a0Masters of Evil\u00a0due to his hatred for\u00a0Iron Man.
