"{{Infobox character\n|image = Mandrake the Malfeasant.png\n|name = Mandrake the Malfeasant\n|shows = ''[[Wander Over Yonder]]''\n|voice = [[Keith Ferguson]]\n|inspiration = [[wikipedia:Ming the Merciless|Ming the Merciless]]<m>[[Maleficent]]\n|personality = Forgetful, feeble, sickly\n|alignment = Bad\n|home = His ship\n|minions = His robots\n|enemies = [[Stella Starbella]], [[Wander]], [[Sylvia]], [[Lord Hater]], [[Lord Dominator]]}}\n'''Mandrake the Malfeasant'''\u00a0is a character from ''[[Wander Over Yonder]]''.\n\n==Background==\nHe is an elderly super villain and one of the top ten villains in the galaxy according to the\u00a0Galactic Villain Leaderboard\u00a0with 15 planets under his rule. Despite his feeble and weak persona, he was once the most feared and powerful villain in the galaxy, as well as the arch-nemesis of the galaxy's former greatest heroine,\u00a0Stella Starbella.\n\n===Personality===\nMandrake acts like a senile old man, usually appearing quite forgetful and feeble, as well as sickly to the point where he may even appear as though he is gravely ill or dying. Despite this, he is still a very evil and competent villain who, while having fallen from the top spot in his old age, is still one of the most notorious villains in the galaxy.\n\n==Relationships==\n===Stella Starbella===\nStarbella Poster\nA young Mandrake in a Starbella poster.\nEven though they are mortal enemies with an incredibly long history of conflict, Mandrake is in fact in love with Starbella and deeply regrets that her heart was the one thing he could not conquer.\n\n==Trivia==\n*He is based on [[wikipedia:Ming the Merciless|Ming the Merciless]], the main villain from the ''[[wikipedia:Flash Gordon|Flash Gordon]]'' franchise.\n*The name \"Mandrake\" comes from [[wikipedia:Mandrake the Magician|Mandrake the Magician]], another King Features Syndicate character.
