Loone is a character in Breath of the Wild.


== Biography ==
Loone is a character who resides on Puffer Beach. She has a crazed personality and is obsessed with Sheikah technology. When Link first encounters her, she is seen caressing an Ancient Orb which she calls Roscoe. In the Guardian Slideshow side quest, Link must show her various pictures of different types of Guardians, in order to get Roscoe from her possession.
