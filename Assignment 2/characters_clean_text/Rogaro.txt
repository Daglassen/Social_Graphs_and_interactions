Rogaro is a character in Breath of the Wild.


== Biography ==
Rogaro is the owner of the Armor shop Ripped and Shredded in Goron City. He sells the Flamebreaker Set, and highly recommends it to Link if he intends on scaling Death Mountain. Rogaro states that the Armor he sells are intended for tourists, as Gorons do not normally wear clothes.
