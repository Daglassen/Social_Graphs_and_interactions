However, after her criminal ties with her family shattered her political career, she murders Cornell in rage and succeeds him in his criminal empire. She soon became the second biggest crime boss in New York, controlling most of the organized crimes and gangs that the [[Kingpin]] doesn't control. She had her own enforcer and consigliere (number one man) in [[Shades]]. Just like Kingpin is opposed by [[Daredevil]], Mariah is opposed by [[Luke Cage]], who continually fights to bring Mariah to justice and take down her criminal empire.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Mariah Dillard marks the fifth time Alfre Woodard played a character in Marvel Comics production, the first three times were Dondi Reese, the Queen Mother and a Dora Milaje in the animated series ''The Black Panther'', the fourth time was an angry mother in ''[[Captain America: Civil War]]''.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\" spacing=\"small\">\nMariah Dillard.jpg\nBlack Mariah2.jpg\nBlack Mariah3.jpg|Mariah Dillard receiving an interview\nBlack Mariah.png\nBlack Mariah and Cottonmouth.jpg|Dillard talking with Stokes\nBlack Mariah4.jpg|Dillard's disastrous interview\nBlack Mariah5.jpg|Dillard finds out about [[Cottonmouth]]'s arrest\nBlack Mariah6.jpg|Dillard confronts Cottonmouth\nBlack Mariah2.png|Dillard hounded by reporters\nBlack Mariah7.jpg|Dillard after she knocks her cousin out the window\nLuke Cage - 2x01 - Soul Brother 1 - Photography - Shades, Mariah and Piranha Meeting.jpg\nLuke Cage - 2x04 - I Get Physical - Photogrpahy - Mariah and Bushmaster.jpg\nLuke Cage - 2x05 - All Souled Out - Shades and Mariah.png\nLuke Cage - 2x07 - On and On - Photography - Mariah and Tilly.jpg\nLuke Cage - 2x12 - Can't Front On Me - Shades and Mariah.jpg\n</gallery>\n\n==External links==\n*[[w:c:marvelcinematicuniversewikia.com:Black Mariah|Black Mariah in Marvel Cinematic Universe Wiki]]\n\n{{Marvel Cinematic Universe}}\n[[Category:Live-action characters]]\n[[Category:Marvel Comics characters]]\n[[Category:Marvel Cinematic Universe characters]]\n[[Category:Luke Cage characters]]\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Mobsters]]\n[[Category:African American characters]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Politicians]]\n[[Category:Acquired characters]]\n[[Category:American characters]]\n[[Category:Live-action villains]]"
