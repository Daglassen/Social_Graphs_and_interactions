"{{Infobox character\n|image = Frank Stillwell.jpg\n|name = Frank Stillwell\n|films = ''[[The Apple Dumpling Gang]]''\n|actor = [[Slim Pickens]]\n|occupation = Outlaw, leader of the Stillwell gang\n|minions = Minions of the gang\n|enemies = [[Bobby Bradley]], [[Clovis Bradley]], [[Celia Bradley]], [[Magnolia Dusty Clydesdale]], [[Russell Donovan]], Homer McCoy, [[Theodore and Amos]]\n|likes = Robbery\n|dislikes = Getting caught, Theodore and Amos\n|weapons = Gun\n|status = Arrested}}'''Frank Stillwell''' is the main antagonist of ''[[The Apple Dumpling Gang]]''.\n\n==Role in the film==\nFrank is the leader of the Stillwell gang and the former boss of [[Theodore and Amos]], when the latter accidentally shoots him in the leg. He hears about the gold nugget [[Bobby Bradley|Bobby]], [[Clovis Bradley|Clovis]], and [[Celia Bradley]] find and plans to steal it. He impersonates a priest and goes to the bank it's in, but Bobby, Clovis, Celia, Theodore, and Amos get in the run. Because of a lot of gunfire there, he gives it up and lets Theodore and Amos have it. [[Russell Donovan]] and [[Magnolia Dusty Clydesdale]] stop him because he is kidnapping Celia. And in the end, Homer McCoy arrests him.\n\n==Trivia==\n*He is named after the infamous outlaw, [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Frank_Stilwell Frank Stilwell], who was gunned down by the legendary lawman, Wyatt Earp, when he was suspected for the murder of Earp's brother Morgan.
