"[[File:Pitts.jpg|thumb|250px]]\n'''Pitts''' is a character and antagonist from the TV show ''[[Bonkers]]''.\n\n==History==\nHe is a toon dog, and the former co-star of the TV show \"Pitts and Smarts\". After his show and career are cancelled, he threatens a bank with his signature bomb in exchange for his job. After multiple chances gone awry, [[Bonkers D. Bobcat|Bonkers]] blows him up at an airport with his own weapon.
