Mils is a character in Breath of the Wild.


== Biography ==
Mils is a Hylian who hunts for treasure with his sister Mina. The siblings can be found near the Exchange Ruins north of the Great Plateau. The two of them can be attacked by Bokoblins.
Mils wants to go home but his sister demands that they keep searching for treasure.
