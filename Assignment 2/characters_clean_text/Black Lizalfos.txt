Black Lizalfos are recurring Enemies in The Legend of Zelda series.


== Characteristics ==


=== Breath of the Wild ===

Black Lizalfos are a stronger variant of green Lizalfos and Blue Lizalfos. They often carry stronger Lizalfos Weapons. Like most of them, they can be found swimming in bodies of water around the ocean or lakes. They can also be found within Monster Strongholds.
