Savelle is a character in Breath of the Wild.


== Biography ==
Savelle is a traveler who can be found passing through Tabantha Bridge Stable. When Link first encounters him, Savelle shares a rumor about how Bows can inflict Critical Hits if an enemy's head is struck with an Arrow. Savelle wears a Soldier's Helm and owns a white and brown Horse. He will reveal to Link various tidbits of information, but will encourage Link to deny that is was him who told the rumour.[citation needed]
