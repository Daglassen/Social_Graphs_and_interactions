Teebo is a character in Breath of the Wild.


== Biography ==
Teebo is a child living in Hateno Village. He plays by himself near the entrance to Hateno Village and will offer to show Link something he found. If Link accepts, Teebo will guide him along the path to Firly Pond and show Link the Horned Statue, claiming that it looks like the Goddess Statue at Reede's house. If Link speaks to the Horned Statue, Teebo will exclaim Link's weirdness and return to playing in Hateno Village.
