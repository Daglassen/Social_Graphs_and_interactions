"{{Infobox character\n|name=Princess Zaria\n|image=Normal 06r2.jpg\n|shows=''[[The Suite Life on Deck]]''\n|actor=[[Debby Ryan]]\n}}\n'''Princess Zaria''' is a character and antagonist from ''[[The Suite Life on Deck]]''. She appears in the Season 2 episode, \"Can You Dig It?\".\u00a0Her spirit was released from the statue when [[Zack Martin|Zack]] found her crown, and she possessed [[Bailey Pickett|Bailey]] to find it. She is portrayed by [[Debby Ryan]].\n\n==History==\nShe\u00a0is an evil Aztec princess.\u00a0Cody\u00a0has been studying Princess Zaria and gets mad when\u00a0Zack\u00a0takes all the credit. When they find her crown her spirit later takes over\u00a0Bailey's\u00a0body. She then blames Zack for stealing her crown and plans on kidnapping him and having his flesh eaten by red fire ants. When everyone confronts her Cody tries to get to Bailey. They find out Zaria's spirit will return to the dead if they remove her crown and return it to the statue. While Bailey begins to \"fight\" Zaria, Cody returns the crown back. Zack is saved and gives Cody all the credit. Before they return to the S.S. Tipton, London accidentally unleashes Zaria.
