Nack is a character in Breath of the Wild.


== Biography ==
Nack is a farmer who lives in Hateno Village. He can be found tending to his garden near the entrance of the Village. When spoken to, he offers to give Link directions around town. If Link asks for directions to a "strange place," Nack will tell him about the Kochi Dye Shop operated by Sayge. If Link asks for directions to a "very strange place," Nack will direct Link to the Hateno Ancient Tech Lab. If Link asks for a place to rest, Nack will mention the Ton Pu Inn and add that Bolson Construction also offers housing. This directs Link to the Side Quest "Hylian Homeowner", a quest in which Link can purchase a House for himself.
