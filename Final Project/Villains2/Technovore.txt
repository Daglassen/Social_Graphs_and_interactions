"{{Infobox character\n|image = Technovore.jpg\n|name = Technovore\n|shows = ''[[The Avengers: Earth's Mightiest Heroes]]''\n|voice = [[Dwight Schultz]]\n|appearance = Parasitic worm, red eyes, two arms, mouth with teeth with tentacles\n|alliance = A.I.M.\n|enemies = [[Iron Man]]\n|likes = Consuming data, machinery, and energy.\n|powers = Data consuming, Superhuman strength}}\n'''Technovore''' is a nanotech A.I. in the form of a large mechanical parasite that consumes any data, machinery, and energy itself.\n\n==Background==\n===Appearance===\nTechnovore is a large creature towering over people like War Machine. It's a computerized A.I. in the from of a large [[Wikipedia:Parasitic worm|parasitic worm]], it has two red eyes which can detetect data energy. It has two arms which can strength to several meters and a mouth with teeth with tentacles in it to consume energy.\n\n===Personality===\nBeing an A.I. it has no feelings and does what it is programmed to do. Technovore is programmed to consume datas for companies, until it went out of control and consumes everything in its path which it desires without feeling any remorse to it.\n\n===Power and Abilities===\n*'''Data consuming''': Technovore can consume any data, machinery, and energy.\n*'''Superhuman strength''': Technovore has superhuman strength able to hold on his own in battle.\n\n==History==\n===Early life===\nTechnovore was created to destroy any data by an unknown creator, but went out of control and consumed everything it encounters. It was then later captured and send to The Vault by [[S.H.I.E.L.D.]].\n\n===Present===\nWhen the Breakout happened in The Vault, Technovore escaped alongside other criminals. At a certain point it was captured by [[A.I.M.]] and was put in a containment pod.\n\nLater A.I.M. used Technovore to take revenge on [[Iron Man|Tony Stark]]. When A.I.M. released Technovore in [[Stark Industries]], it went after Tony's arc reactor in his body and fought both Tony and [[Maria Hill]]. After War Machine was used by James, Technovore went after him. When Technovore dragged War Machine to the arc reactor, Iron Man in the Mark IX armor used Technovore to consume the energy of the reactor and to deactivate the bombs as well. Technovore then exploded due to consuming too much energy.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Technovore looks a lot different then in the comics, in the comics Technovore has more of a monstrous appearance than a parasitic worm.\n*This is Technovore's second animated appearance.
