"[[File:Klegghorn.jpg|thumb|256px]]\n\n'''Captain Kevin Klegghorn'''<ref>http://www.imdb.com/character/ch0012298/?ref_=tt_cl_t6</ref> is a character in ''[[Mighty Ducks (TV series)|Mighty Ducks]]'' that is introduced as an antagonist, but later reforms and becomes an anti-hero.\n\nHe is a\u00a0police officer for the city of [[Anaheim]] who initially doesn't trust the ducks, particularly due to the lack of evidence that [[Lord Dragaunus|Dragaunus]] even existed in the first place. While he still doesn\u2019t like them, eventually they come to terms when he helped Wildwing retake the Pond when the Saurians took their headquarters.
