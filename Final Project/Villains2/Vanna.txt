"{{Infobox character\n|image = Vanna % 28Character% 29.jpg\n|title1=Vanna\n|goal = To waken her family\n|likes = Werewolves, Cheer, Family\n|dislikes = Zombies (Formerly), Werewolves (Formerly), Willa (Formerly), Distractions.\n|status=Active}}\n\n'''Vanna''' is a new character who appears in ''[[Zombies: Addison's Moonstone Mystery]]''. She is a teen vampire. The characters see her for the first time in the Seabrook High cafeteria.\n\n<br />\n==Appearance==\nVanna has dark hair with small streaks of red on the sides. Her eyes are lined with purple eyeliner and she wears a pendant around her neck. She wears a leather jacket and her eyes are dark and violet.\n==Personality==\nVanna can come off as sweet, but, as revealed in the first season, she has a dark and fierce side. When she doesn't feel safe, or is embarrassed, she can be moody.\n==Trivia==\n\n*Unlike typical vampires, she may not be affected by the sun as evidenced by her being at the football field outside of school. \n*She caused an earthquake to happen.\n*She can hypnotize others and calls it \"Vanna-tizing\".\n**Somehow, she is not able to hypnotize Addison.\n*Vanna has a crystal on her chest which protects her from sunlight and it's been in her family for hundreds of years.\n*Vanna was woken by the comet, but her family was not.\n*Vanna stated she is not a fan of people.\n*Vanna reveals that she Vanna-tized Addison's friends so she could find her lost family.\n*She can turn into a bat which is what vampires do.\n*She really cares about her family and did anything in her power to awaken them from sleep.\n*At the end of Season 1, her and her family fly away into the night sky.\n[[Category:Vampires]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Heroines]]\n[[Category:Reformed characters]]\n[[Category:Teenagers]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Immortal]]\n[[Category:Disney Channel characters]]"
