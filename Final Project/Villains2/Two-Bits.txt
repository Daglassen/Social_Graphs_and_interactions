He uses a vacuum to suck up dropped change from the audience. He appears in the episode \"[[Stand-In Dad]]\".\n\nHis scheme was to steal all the nickels from all the children in the audience to be reunited with the nickel \"Nicky\" he had as a little kid, which he lost forever when his father dropped it in a parking meter. His great-granddaddy David O. Seltzerwater used to tell him. \"Never trust an agent\".
