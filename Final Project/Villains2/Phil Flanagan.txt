"[[File:Halloweentown-High-2004-ScreenShot-13.jpg|thumb|250px|Phil Flanagan in Halloweentown High]]\n'''Principal Phil Flanagan''' is a character in ''[[Halloweentown High]]''. He's a member of the Knights of the Iron Dagger and the Principal of the high school in the film.\u00a0[[Edgar Dalloway]] found him and revealed the past of his family, gaining Phil's help in trying to permanently seal the portal to Halloweentown.
