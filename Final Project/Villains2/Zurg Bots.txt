"{{Group Infobox\n|image = Profile_-_Zurg_Bot.jpg\n|films = ''[[Toy Story 2]]''\n|games = ''[[Toy Story 3: The Video Game]]''<br> ''[[Disney Infinity]]'' series<br>''[[Disney Emoji Blitz]]'' (unplayable)\n|affiliation = [[Emperor Zurg]]\n|goal = To kill [[Buzz Lightyear]] (failed)\n|fate = Gets destroyed by lasers caused by Buzz Lightyear}}The '''Zurg Bots''' are minor antagonists in [[Walt Disney Pictures|Disney]]/[[Pixar]]'s [[1999]] animated film ''[[Toy Story 2]]'' and the secondary antagonists of the Toy Story in Space ''[[Disney Infinity]]'' playset. They serve as the robot henchmen of [[Emperor Zurg]].\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[Toy Story 2]]''===\nDuring the video game opening, the bots appear when [[Buzz Lightyear]] lands on Zurg's planet. Surrounding him in all sides, the bots wield out their laser guns and weapon and tiny versions of themselves sprouting out from their shoulder armors. Thinking very fast, Buzz spots a giant crystal and shoots it with his laser, and sending hundreds of lasers through the robots. This causes a chain reaction which not only strike the robots, but also caused them to engulf in a large explosion to which Buzz nearly survives.\n\n==Video games==\n===''[[Disney Infinity]]''===\nThey also appear in the Toy Story in Space ''Disney Infinity'' playset.
