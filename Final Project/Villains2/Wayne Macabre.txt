"\n[[File:Wayne_Macabre.jpg|thumb]]'''Wayne Macabre''' is a student at [[Hazelnut Middle School]] who DJ's the school radio station KHMS.<ref> http://pepperann.wikia.com/wiki/Wayne_Macabre</ref>\n\n==Background==\nHe first appears in \"[[Radio Freak Hazelnut]]\" when Pepper Ann accidentally tunes into his radio broadcast during class whilst trying to listen to a national radio station in order to be the tenth called and win Flaming Snot tickets.
