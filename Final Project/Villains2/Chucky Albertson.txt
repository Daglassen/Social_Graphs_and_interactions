"All info was provided:\n[https://iminthebandironweaselband.fandom.com/wiki/Chucky http://iminthebandironweaselband.wikia.com/wiki/Chucky]\n\n{{Infobox character\n|image = Slap Goes the Weasel3.jpg\n|name = Chucky Albertson\n|shows = ''[[I'm in the Band]]''\n|actor = Zayne Emory\n|alignment = Bad|enemies = Tripp, Burger, Ash, Derek|dislikes = Getting tickled}}\n'''Charles \"Chucky\" Albertson''' is a twelve-year-old boy who got injured trying to copy Iron Weasel's signature move, the move, the \"Weasel Slap\", in ''[[I'm in the Band]]''<span style=\"font-weight:normal;line-height:1.5em;\">.</span><span style=\"line-height:1.5em;\">\u00a0He hates being called \"Chucky\", due to it rhyming with Sucky. He likes\u00a0[[Izzy Fuentes|Izzy]]\u00a0because she reminds him of his mother. Charles tried to to get revenge on Iron Weasel for humiliating him on live TV, by calling him \"Chucky\", causing him to throw a temper tantrum. Since Iron Weasel has a record deal with his stepfather, he appears more often.</span>\n\nHe first appears when Iron Weasel creates a move that injures him, and they were forced to obey him due to not wanting more bad publicity, until they make him throw a tantrum on live TV, by calling him \"Chucky\" in their song.\n\nHe later appears when they are babysitting him, and they almost lose their recording deal because they don't have a song to record for their album, but he just ends up giving them an idea for the song to save their recording deal (and [[Tripp Campbell|Tripp]] calls him \"Chucky\").\n\nHe makes a 3rd appearance when they babysit him again, but he goes missing, and they must find him due to thinking that Simon knew he ran away.\n\nHe makes a 4th appearance when they torture him because they think he stole their advance money for their new album.\n\n==Trivia==\n*He's Simon Craig's stepson.\n*He had his first appearance in \"Slap Goes the Weasel\".\n*He returns in \"Chucky's Revenge\".\n*His enemies are Tripp, Burger, [[Ash Tyler|Ash]], and [[Derek Jupiter|Derek]].\n*He makes a cameo in \"Trippnotized\".\n\n==References==\n<references/>\n{{CharacterStub}}\n[[Category:I'm in the Band characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Musicians]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Live-action characters]]\n[[Category:Pre-teens]]\n[[Category:American characters]]\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Live-action villains]]"
