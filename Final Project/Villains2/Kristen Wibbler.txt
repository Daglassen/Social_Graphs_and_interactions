"[[File:Th_555780660_CarolineSunshineSammiHanratty_ShakeItUpS03E1613_122_368lo.jpg|thumb|280px]]'''Kristin Wibbler''' is a one-time character and [[Tinka Hessenheffer]]'s rival who appears in the \"Bag It Up\" episode of ''[[Shake It Up]]''. She was played by [[Sammi Hanratty]].\n\n==History==\nShe first appeared after mocking Tinka's fashion where afterwards Tinka, Rocky and Cece agreed to rent a designer purse.
