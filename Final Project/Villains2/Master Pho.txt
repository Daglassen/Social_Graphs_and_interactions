"[[File:Master_Pho.jpg|thumb|168px|Master Pho as he appears in Season 1]]'''Master Pho''' was Agent K's karate teacher in ''[[The Replacements]]'', but he turned out to be evil and was defeated by Agent K.\n\n==History==\nHe first appeared in \"Master Pho\" when Todd replaced his karate teacher with him and realizes he's one of Agent K's enemies. He later returned in \"Puzzle Me Daring\" wearing a disguise stealing many things like artwork and money, leaving riddles that nobody could solve \"except for Todd\" and together Agent K and Todd defeated Master Pho.
