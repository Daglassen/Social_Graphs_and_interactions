"[[File:Quackshot088.png|thumb|250px]] \n'''King Garuzia''' is the main antagonist of the [[1991]] Disney platforming video game ''[[QuackShot]]''.\n\n==History==\nKing Garuzia once was the ruler of a great duck kingdom. Donald finds the map to King Garuzia's treasure and tries to get it, facing several dangers on his way. When Donald reaches the treasure vault, King Garuzia awakens, forcing Donald to fight.
