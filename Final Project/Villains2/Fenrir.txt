"{{Infobox character\n|image = Fenrir.png\n|shows = ''[[Hercules (TV series)|Hercules]]''\n|personality= Wild, ferocious\n|occupations = Harbinger of Ragnarok\n|alignment = Bad\n|affiliations = [[Loki (Hercules)|Loki]]\n|goal = To consume the sun and start Ragnarok\n|home = Niflheim\n|enemies = [[Hercules (character)|Hercules]], [[Thor (Hercules)|Thor]], [[Odin (Hercules)|Odin]]\n|fate = Is locked away along with Loki for eternity}}'''Fenrir''' is a minor villain in ''[[Hercules: The Animated Series]]''. He is the Harbinger of Ragnarok.\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[Hercules: The Animated Series]]''===\nFenrir appears as the secondary villain-of-the-week of the episode ''[[Hercules and the Twilight of the Gods]]'', where he is freed from his icy prison by Loki's trickery and initiates Ragnarok. Soon after, Fenrir proceeds to freeze the rest of the Asgardian pantheon and helped Loki conquer Asgard. He was defeated by Hercules and locked away along with Loki for presumably eternity.\n\n==Powers and Abilities==\n*Ability to freeze other beings (deity or mortal) with his breath.\n*Ability to consume celestial bodies such as the sun.\n\n==Trivia==\n*In Norse Mythology, the Giant Wolf Fenrir is one of Loki's monstrous children along with the Midgard Serpent and the beautiful/ugly hybrid humanoid Hel.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery>\nFenrir 001.PNG\n</gallery>\n\n{{Hercules}}\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Hercules characters]]\n[[Category:Monsters]]\n[[Category:Immortal]]\n[[Category:Norse Mythology]]\n[[Category:Legendary creatures]]\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Wolves]]"
