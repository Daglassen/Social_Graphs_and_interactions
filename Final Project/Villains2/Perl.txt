"{{Infobox character\n|name = Perl\n|image = Perl.png\n|shows = ''[[Tron: Uprising|TRON: Uprising]]''\n|voice = [[Kate Mara]]\n|status=Incarcerated}}\n'''Perl''' is a minor antagonist of the [[Disney XD]] animated series, ''[[Tron: Uprising]]''.\n\n==History==\nIn the episode\u00a0[[The Renegade|\"The Renegade, Part 1]]\", she meets Zed at a club and tricks\u00a0him into giving her a tour of [[Able]]'s garage. Zed shows her\u00a0Able's office, where he has his vintage [[Light Cycles|light cycle]]\u00a0[[Baton|baton]]. She tricks Zed again, this time asking him to take a look at her light cycle. He steals the vintage light cycle and Zed calls himself an idiot (to which [[Bit]]\u00a0agrees). Zed, with [[Mara]]'s help, tracks Perl and the light cycle down to the docks where Gage, the leader of their gang, takes the cycle for a ride.
