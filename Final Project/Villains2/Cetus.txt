"{{Infobox character|image=Cetus Hercules.png|name=Cetus|films=''[[Hercules (film)|Hercules]]''|inspiration=[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cetus_(mythology) Cetus]|dislikes=[[Hercules (character)|Hercules]]|status=Deceased}}The '''Cetus''' is a minor antagonist from the [[1997]] animated film ''[[Hercules (film)|Hercules]]''.\n\n==Role in [[Hercules (film)|''Hercules'']]==\nIn the Hercules film, the Cetus is one of the many giant monsters that appears during the song [[Zero to Hero]]. He appears while [[Hercules (character)|Hercules]] and [[Philoctetes]] were rowing a boat through a body of water. While Philoctetes coward in fear, Hercules grabbed the serpent by the throat and threw him around like a wet towel. The scene then changed to a vase depicting Hercules holding the skeleton of the beast. \n\nIn another scene, [[Hades]] was seen melting a small pawn piece of the creature in his hand out of anger. \n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=center>\nCetus Defeated.png|The Cetus being defeated. \nCetus Hercules Defeated.png|The deceased Cetus painted on a vase. \nCetus Piece.png|The Cetus's pawn piece being melted. \n</gallery>\n\n==Trivia==\n*The Cetus is one of the few characters to not have been brought back in any of the Hercules media that followed the film.
