Sudrey is a character in Breath of the Wild.


== Biography ==
Sudrey is a Gerudo soldier who can be found at the Lookout Post in the Gerudo Desert. She was ordered by the Gerudo Chief, Riju, to monitor the Divine Beast Vah Naboris from the Post. If Link speaks to Sudrey, she says that a Hylian is a rare sight and warns him to stay away from Vah Naboris.
If Link speaks to Sudrey after calming Vah Naboris, she explains that although the Divine Beast has been calmed, the monsters around the Gerudo Desert are still a problem. Sudrey continues to patrol the Desert from the Lookout Post to keep an eye on everything.
