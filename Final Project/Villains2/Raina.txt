She also recruited [[Michael Peterson]] into the [[Centipede Project]]. She convinced Miles Lydon\u00a0to hack into the\u00a0[[Hong Kong]]\u00a0[[S.H.I.E.L.D.]]\u00a0database, paying him one million dollars. She kidnaps [[Chan Ho Yin]]\u00a0and brings him back to the lab to test his abilities under the influence of the [[Extremis]]\u00a0serum.\n\nAt the end of \"[[Girl in the Flower Dress]]\" she talks to Edison Po in prison and tells him he has to talk to \"[[John Garrett|the Clairvoyant]]\". She makes another appearance in \"[[The Bridge]]\" where she has recruited more super-soldiers for the centipede project. She has Edison Po broken out of prison for his ability to communicate with the Clairvoyant. She kidnaps [[Ace Peterson]]\u00a0and exchanges him safely, so that she can take [[Phil Coulson|Agent Coulson]]\u00a0captive. She put Coulson to a Theta Brain-Wave Frequency Machine to uncover lost memories of Project T.A.H.I.T.I. Raina was later arrested and sent to prison.\n\nShe was later freed by [[Grant Ward]]. When she met [[John Garrett]], she was disappointed that Garrett was no clairvoyant and that Garrett wanted the GH-325 for himself. She synthesized a copy of the drug and injected it into Garrett. After Garrett's downfall, Raina came across Calvin Zabo and gave him a picture of Skye.\n\nIn \"[[Face My Enemy]]\", Raina goes to [[Florida]] to find a painting, she at the get into his car meets Whitehall who informs her that if she does not return the [[Obelisk]] in forty-eight hours he personally will to torture her.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Raina who is an original character, created for ''[[Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.]]'', was adapted into the mainstream continuity, of [[Marvel Comics]].\n**Others elements from the series that were adapted include: [[Melinda May]], [[Grant Ward]], [[Leo Fitz]], [[Jemma Simmons]], [[Jiaying]], [[Gordon (Marvel)|Gordon]], Katya Belyakov and [[Lola]].\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery spacing=\"small\" position=\"center\" widths=\"150\" captionalign=\"center\">\nAgents of S.H.I.E.L.D. - 2x22 - S.O.S.
