(TV series)|W.I.T.C.H.]]''\n\n==Chronology==\nTynar is a castle guard for [[Prince Phobos|Phobos]]. When Cedric, he and the other guards tried to capture Caleb and Blunk, he was severely wounded and left behind to rot. The Guardians decided to take him to Earth to cure him. For a reward, he decides to join the rebels and spread the knowledge that what Phobos had told them were lies. He guides Caleb and the girls to the prison cell where Aldarn, Vathek, Julian and where a lot of rebels were being held. Tynar keeps true to his word and spreads the truth around. He even convinces some guards to join the rebels who help the Guardians and the rebel army defeat Phobos and save Elyon.\n\nIn the second season, he becomes a part-time sentry to Phobos and was attacked by Nerissa. He also leads the work crew to rebuild the village next to the castle but is attacked and captured by the Knights of Vengeance to Drake's dismay. Later the Guardians are able to free him. After Phobos defeats Nerissa and sizes her Seal he helps the Guardians fight against him and his followers.
