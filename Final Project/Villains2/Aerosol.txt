She is a mermaid\u00a0who appears\u00a0in the episode\u00a0\"A Mermaid's Tail\". She has brown hair and an orange tail.\n\n===Appearance===\nAerosol is a beautiful woman with light skin and long light brown hair with strands of orange fabric. She wears a bright orange top with strands of beads and a starfish necklace with a gemstone in its center. Her tail is bright orange.\n\n===Personality===\nAerosol is the leader of the mermaids and a natural leader and an manipulative but sly mermaid. She is well aware of her enchanting beauty and will use it to deceive men. She is wicked and selfish who cares only about herself.
