It is an adversary to the Norse god [[Thor Odinson|Thor]]. A live-action version of the character appears in ''[[Thor: Ragnarok]]''.\n\nThis version of the mythological Fenris Wolf was created by [[Stan Lee]] and [[Jack Kirby]].\n\n==Appearances==\n===[[Marvel Cinematic Universe]]===\n====''[[Thor: Ragnarok]]''====\nFenris wolf appears in the film as an ally to [[Hela]], although this version of the character is female. She is the loyal pet of\u00a0Hela, and is resurrected alongside Hela's army to aid Hela in ruling Asgard. Later, Fenris battles the\u00a0[[Hulk]]\u00a0and after a fierce brawl, gets tossed off the side of Asgard to her death into the void below.\n\n===Marvel Animated Universe===\n====''[[The Avengers: Earth's Mightiest Heroes]]''====\nThe Fenris Wolf appears in the episode \"Meet Captain America\". It was one of the mythical specimens that were captured by [[Red Skull]] upon discovering an entrance to [[Asgard]]. Due to a control collar around its neck, the wolf was forced to obey Red Skull; after it is released however, it ends up fighting [[Captain America]] and [[Bucky Barnes|Bucky]].\n\n====''[[Ultimate Spider-Man]]''====\nThe Fenris Wolf appears in the two-part episode \"Avenging Spider-Man\" as a host for the [[Venom]] symbiote among the other Asgardian creatures as part of a takeover conducted by [[Loki Laufeyson|Loki]] and [[Doctor Octopus]]. After [[Spider-Man]] switches minds with Loki, the Venom-wolf attacks Spider-Man in Loki's body until it is blasted away by Loki's scepter. It runs into Spider-Man once again (after Spider-Man had returned to own body). This time, Spider-Man was aided by [[Sam Wilson|Falcon]], so they managed to remove the symbiote from Fenris and shock it into unconsciousness. After Loki is transferred to another realm, the Fenris Wolf, [[Ulik]], and the rest of the Asgardian creatures confront Loki as revenge for Loki controlling them.\n\n====''[[Avengers Assemble]]''====\nIn the episode \"A Friend in Need\", the wolf is shown incarcerated in Asgard prison, while its history with Tyr is briefly mentioned by Thor.\n\n==Trivia==\n*In Marvel Comics and in Norse mythology, Fenris and [[Hela]] are actually [[Loki Laufeyson|Loki]]'s children.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\" captionalign=\"center\" widths=\"185\">\nAvengers Assemble Fenris.png|Fenris Wolf in ''Avengers Assemble''\n</gallery>\n\n==External links==\n*{{Interwiki|https://vignette3.wikia.nocookie.net/marvelcinematicuniverse/images/6/64/Favicon.ico|marvelcinematicuniverse|Fenris|Marvel Cinematic Universe Wiki}}\n*{{Interwiki|https://vignette3.wikia.nocookie.net/marveldatabase/images/6/64/Favicon.ico|marvel|Fenris Wolf|Marvel Comics Database}}\n\n{{Marvel Cinematic Universe}}\n{{Marvel Animated Universe}}\n[[Category:Wolves]]\n[[Category:Legendary creatures]]\n[[Category:Thor characters]]\n[[Category:Marvel Cinematic Universe characters]]\n[[Category:Marvel Comics characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Mystical animals]]\n[[Category:Ultimate Spider-Man characters]]\n[[Category:Avengers Assemble characters]]\n[[Category:The Avengers: Earth's Mightiest Heroes characters]]\n[[Category:Live-action characters]]\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Live-action villains]]\n[[Category:Giants]]\n[[Category:Norse Mythology]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Deceased characters]]\n[[Category:Acquired characters]]\n[[Category:Disney XD characters]]"
