He, along with with the rest of the members, has his memories erased.\n\nWhen Mabel and Dipper traveled back to the Gravity Falls of ten years prior in \"[[Blendin's Game]]\", Tats was seen at the Tattoo parlor, apparently receiving the first of his many tattoos.\n\nIn \"[[A Tale of Two Stans]]\", a young tattooless Tats is seen buying groceries at the\u00a0Dusk 2 Dawn\u00a0convenience store. He has no lines and appears greatly different, being several inches shorter in height, with dark hair and a flat top cut and wearing a green polo shirt. He is even smiling. Because of this he is barely recognizable, save for his head and facial structure.\n\nIn \"[[Weirdmageddon Part 2: Escape from Reality|Weirdmageddon 2: Escape From Reality]]\", he is one of the people in\u00a0Bill's\u00a0throne.\n\nIn \"[[Weirdmageddon Part 3: Take Back the Falls|Weirdmageddon 3: Take Back The Falls]]\", he is seen being freed from Bill Cipher's throne and escapes with the all other residents afterwords.\n\n== Appearance ==\nHe is a large, muscular thug with dark skin, a deep voice, and many tattoos. On his forehead, there is a tattoo that says \"HEAD\", on his chin, there is a tattoo that says \"CHIN\", and on the back of his neck there is one that says \"NECK.\" He also has a tattoo on his shoulder that says \"Over The Shoulder\", one that says \"No \u2764\ufe0f for U\", and another of a swirling square. He also has nose piercings and a unibrow.
