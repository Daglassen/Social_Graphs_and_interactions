"[[File:Kalidah.jpg|thumb|250px]] \n'''The Kalidah Critics''' are a pair of vicious creatures who rip those they encounter to shreds, not physically, but through their words. They have hairy, clawed bodies and horns on their grizzled heads. The pair guard the Kalidah Bridge, which must be crossed on the way to the [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Emerald_City Emerald City].\n\n==Behind the Scenes==\nThe Kalidah Critics are loosely based on the Kalidahs, dangerous animals with the bodies of bears and the heads of tigers, as found in the original ''[http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Wizard_of_Oz Oz]'' books by L. Frank Baum.
