Smaude is a character in Breath of the Wild.


== Biography ==
Smaude is a Gerudo soldier who can be found in the barracks in Gerudo Town. If Link speaks to her, she comments about how the Yiga Clan has guts to scheme against them and that they would lose in a fair fight. Smaude then mutters under her breath that the training is tough. If Link speaks to Smaude after recovering the Thunder Helm, she complains about Babi pushing them to train without rest and wishes Babi could feel how tired she is.
