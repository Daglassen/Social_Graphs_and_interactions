Peeks is a character in Breath of the Wild.[citation needed]


== Biography ==
Peeks is a Korok found inside The Great Deku Tree in the Korok Forest. He stands near a Cooking Pot in the Tree. When Link speaks to Peeks, he can begin the "Legendary Rabbit Trial" Side Quest to find a Picture of a Blupee and show it to Peeks.
