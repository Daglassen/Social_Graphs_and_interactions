"[[File:Grodin.jpg|thumb|250px]] \n'''Quentin Fitzwaller''' is a [[Walt Disney World]] security guard and antagonist in ''[[The Muppets at Walt Disney World]]''. He was played by Charles Grodin. Out of fear of having to scrape spearmint off of park benches for the rest of his career, he enlists [[Rizzo the Rat|Rizzo]] to help track down the members of \"[[The Muppets]]\", who have neglected to pay the price of admission for the theme park.\n\nThough he manages to catch the \"Kismet Gang\" and turn them over to [[Mickey Mouse]], he was seen in the \"[[We're Not Gonna Stop]]\" song scraping spearmint from the bottom of the benches.\n{{MuppetsWiki}}\n{{DEFAULTSORT:Fitzwaller, Quentin}}\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Live-action characters]]\n[[Category:Police officers]]\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Live-action villains]]\n[[Category:Main antagonists]]"
