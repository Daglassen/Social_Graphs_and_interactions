"{{Expand}}\n{{Infobox character\n|image = Dang Hu.jpg\n|films = ''[[Raya and the Last Dragon]]''\n|voice = [[Lucille Soong]]\n|occupation = Chieftess of Talon\n|home = [[Talon (Raya and the Last Dragon)|Talon Land]], [[Kumandra]]}}\n'''Dang Hu''' is a minor antagonist in [[Walt Disney Animation Studios|Disney]]'s [[2021]] animated feature film, ''[[Raya and the Last Dragon]]''. She served as the elderly chieftess of [[Talon (Raya and the Last Dragon)|Talon]] in [[Kumandra]], during the [[Druun]] calamity. Cunning and villainous, Dang Hu used the Drunn to advance her power and assert her authority over her enemies.\n\n==Background==\n===Physical Appearance===\nDang Hu is an elderly Asian woman who wears the purple and pink color scheme clothing of her Kumandra tribe, Talon.\n\n==Appearance==\nAs the chieftess of Talon, a floating city that has managed to prosper since the Druun is afraid of water, Dang Hu might seem like a harmless elderly woman, but she's as sinister as they come. In fact, she's the only one who used the Druun to her advantage by locking intruders in a room with Druun to be turned to stone.\n\n{{CharacterStub}}\n{{Raya and the Last Dragon}}\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Raya and the Last Dragon characters]]\n[[Category:Elderly characters]]\n[[Category:Nobility]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Characters in the Disney animated features canon]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Villains]]"
