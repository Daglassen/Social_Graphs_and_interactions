Danton is a character in Breath of the Wild.[name reference needed]


== Biography ==
Danton is a resident of the Snowfield Stable. During the day, he will sit beside the Cooking Pot, that belongs to him, next to the Stable. He will quickly move into the stable if it starts snowing. Danton is enamored with the Sword that Seals the Darkness, thinking about it even while sleeping. If Link has obtained the Master Sword, he can show it to Danton. After Danton calms down, he will note that the Sword is not as big or extravagant as he thought it would be, but he still finds it amazing and says that he will never forget the day that Link showed him the Master Sword.
