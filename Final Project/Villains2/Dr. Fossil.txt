He is the creator of [[Stegmutt]].\n==Background==\nDr. Fossil was once a paleontologist who worked at the Natural History Museum in [[St. Canard]]. Due to his love of prehistoric creatures, he turned himself into a pterosaur (possibly a Pteranodon) using a Retro-Evolution Gun. Before long, he turned a number of others into dinosaurs.
