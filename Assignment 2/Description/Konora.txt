She lives in [[Gerudo Town]], but she is stuck at the Kara Kara Bazaar because of the threat of the {{Term|BotW|Divine Beast Vah Naboris|link}} and its lightning.<ref>{{Cite|If only that {{Color|BotW Blue|Divine Beast}} wasn't around. That {{Color|BotW Blue|Divine Beast}}... I can't risk traveling to {{Color|BotW Blue|Gerudo Town}}...|Konora|BotW}}</ref> If Link asks about Gerudo Town, Konora explains that it is a large, lively town to the southwest made up almost entirely of Gerudo people.<ref>{{Cite|There's a town to the southwest, made up almost entirely of Gerudo.|Konora|BotW}}</ref><ref>{{Cite|It's the largest settlement in these parts.
