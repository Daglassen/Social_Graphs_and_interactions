(Evil Laughter)\"|likes = Shiny things|personality = Rude with an occasional soft side}}'''The Wicked King''' is a character in ''[[Doc McStuffins]]'', and the main antagonist/villain of the toys with a Posh English accent. He\u00a0always plays as the villain in every playtime. While he can be mean, he does care about the other toys and doesn't want others to get hurt. The Wicked King's real name is Percy which was mentioned in \"[[Wrap It Up]]\". His first friend was [[Sproingo Boingo]] and [[Queen Amena]], who he shares the toy castle with him.\n\n\n\n== Appearance ==\nThe Wicked King or Percy is a king with a crown on his head, purple body and has brown hair.
