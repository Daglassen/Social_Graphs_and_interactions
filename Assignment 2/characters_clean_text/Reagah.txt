Reagah is a character in Breath of the Wild.


== Biography ==
Reagah is a Goron who can be found in the Hammerhead in Zora's Domain. He was originally recruited by Sidon to help calm Divine Beast Vah Ruta.. However, Sidon deemed him too heavy to carry, so he ended up abandoning that plan.
When asked by Link, Reagah will describe Goron City, Death Mountain, and Goron diets.
