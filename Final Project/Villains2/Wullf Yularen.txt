"{{Infobox character\n|image = Yularen Original.png\n|films = ''Star Wars: A New Hope''\n|shows = ''[[Star Wars: The Clone Wars]]<br>[[Star Wars Rebels]]''\n|actor = Robert Clarke\n|voice = [[Tom Kane]]\n|alias = Admiral Yularen<br>Colonel Yularen\n|occupation = Admiral of the Republic Navy (formerly)<br>Colonel of the Imperial Security Bureau (formerly)<br>Teacher (formerly)<br>Deputy Director of the Naval Intelligence Agency\n|affiliations = [[Galactic Republic]] (formerly)<br>[[Galactic Empire]]\n|home = [[Coruscant]]\n|family = \n|likes =Order, serving the Republic (formerly), the Empire \n|dislikes =Traitors, rebels, disorder \n|status = Deceased\n|quote = }}\n'''Wullf Yularen''' is a character in the ''[[Star Wars]]'' saga, who made a minor appearance in ''A New Hope''. In ''[[Star Wars: The Clone Wars]]'' he is a recurring character, and he appeared in the show's successor, ''[[Star Wars Rebels]]''.\n\nDuring the Clone Wars, Yularen served as an Admiral in the [[Galactic Republic]] Navy and later became a Colonel in the [[Galactic Empire]]'s Imperial Security Bureau. Eventually, Yularen was promoted to Deputy Director of the Naval Intelligence Agency and was one of the officers assigned to the [[Death Star]] where during the Battle of Yavin, he was killed when the battle station was destroyed.\n\n==Film Appearances==\n===''Star Wars: A New Hope''===\nYularen's appearance is brief in the first ''Star Wars'' movie. He is first seen in the conference room aboard the Death Star. He perished in the destruction of the Death Star.\n\n==Television Appearances==\n===''[[Star Wars: The Clone Wars]]''===\nIn the series, Yularen appeared as a recurring character in the series, voiced by [[Tom Kane]]. He serves as an Admiral during the Clone Wars for the Republic, operating under [[Jedi|Jedi General]] [[Anakin Skywalker]] frequently. In the series, Yularen is portrayed as a by-the-book operator, who was often frustrated with Anakin's recklessness.\n\n===''[[Star Wars Rebels]]''===\nYularen appears in the ''Star Wars Rebels'' episode, \"[[Through Imperial Eyes]]\", where by now he has become a Colonel in the Imperial Security Bureau following the reformation of the Galactic Republic into the Galactic Empire.\n\n==Trivia==\n*In the first ''Star Wars'' movie, Wullf Yularen was an unnamed character portrayed by Robert Clarke.
