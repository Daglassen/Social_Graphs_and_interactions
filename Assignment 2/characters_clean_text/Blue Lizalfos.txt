Blue Lizalfos are recurring Enemies in The Legend of Zelda series.


== Characteristics ==


=== Breath of the Wild ===

Blue Lizalfos are stronger than green Lizalfos, as they have more health and carry stronger Weapons. Like other Lizalfos, they can be often be found in or around bodies of water, such as Lakes and Rivers, but they can also be found throughout the Gerudo Desert.
