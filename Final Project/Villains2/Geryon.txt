"[[File:Geryon.jpg|thumb|250px|Geryon]]\n'''Geryon''' is a character from the\u00a0''[[Hercules (TV series)|Hercules]]''\u00a0TV series. He appears in the episode \u00a8Hercules and the Jilt Trip.\u00a8\n\n==History==\nHe is a large human-like monster with three torsos. Unlike the rest of his multi-headed siblings, Geryon's heads think in unison rather than each head having a mind of its own. He used to be the boyfriend of [[Lavina|Princess Lavinia]], but after their relationship ended, he became overly emotional and prone to fits of sobbing when he thinks about her. He even develops a friendship with [[Hercules (character)|Hercules]] when he finds out that he also got dumped by his girlfriend. Later on Geryon discovers that Lavinia is in love with Hercules and believes that Hercules betrayed him causing him to fight Hercules in a blind rage. Luckily Hercules convinces Lavinia to get back together with Geryon thus restoring their relationship.
