He is also commander of the Third Systems Army, 7th Sky Corps, and Ghost Company.\n\n==Background==\n===Abilities===\n*'''Expert Hand-To-Hand Combatant:''' As a Clone Commander, Cody is extremely skilled in unarmed combat.\n*'''Expert Marksman:''' As a Clone Commander, Cody is extremely skilled in utilizing blaster pistols and blaster rifles.\n*'''High-Level Intellect/Expert Tactician/Leader:''' Cody is highly intelligent. As a Clone Commander, Cody is an extremely skilled tactician and a capable leader.\n\n===Weapons and Equipment===\n====Armor====\n*'''Phase II Clone Trooper armor:''' Cody utilizes Phase II Clone Trooper armor to protect himself from his enemies in battle.\n\n===Former Weapons and Equipment===\n====Armor====\n*'''Phase I Clone Trooper armor:''' Cody utilized Phase I Clone Trooper armor to protect himself from his enemies in battle.\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[Star Wars: The Clone Wars]]''===\nLike most clone troopers, Cody was born on [[Kamino]]. Cody was involved in many battles and conflicts in the war though he did not participate in the First Battle of Geonosis but he did participate in many others alongside [[Obi-Wan Kenobi|Obi Wan Kenobi]].  Such as the Battle of Christophisis, Battle of Teth, Second Battle of Geonosis, Battle of Ryloth, Battle of Selucime, Battle of Umbara, and Battle for Anaxes.\n\n===''Revenge of the Sith''===\nLater in the Clone Wars, during the Battle of Utapau, [[Obi-Wan Kenobi]] and Cody lead the 212th Attack Battalion with the aim of capturing/destroying [[General Grievous]].
