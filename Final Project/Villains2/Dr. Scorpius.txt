Looks like your snooping kids have made a pit-stop!''\"\n|image = Dr. Scorpius.jpg}}'''Dr. Scorpius''' is [[Agent K]]'s arch-nemesis in ''[[The Replacements]]''. He has a scorpion tail-shaped beard and is usually seen trying to take over the world in some way. He's also known for speaking with a lisp In \"The Spy Who Wasn't Riley,\", he tried to destroy Antarctica with a giant laser cannon. In another episode, he was seen relaxing on a beach that Todd and Riley happened to be on at the time. In \"The Rizzle\", Todd finds out that he needs glasses, but Agent K thinks he has been infected with \"Dr. Scorpius' Genetically Crafted Island Eye-Fog\". Appeared in \"The Spy That\u00a0Wasn't Riley\", \"Canadian Fakin'\", \"Glee by the Sea\", and \"Irreplaceable\".
