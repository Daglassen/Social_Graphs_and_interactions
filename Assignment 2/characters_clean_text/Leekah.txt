Leekah is a character in Breath of the Wild.


== Biography ==
Leekah is a young woman who likes to watch the river by the Woodland Stable. There she is often attacked by monsters in the area. If Link saves her, she expresses her gratitude by giving him a small gift she had intended to give to another. Afterwards, she leaves to return to the Woodland Stable. Leekah is prone to becoming irritable and bossy after being attacked, especially if Link fails to save her from being knocked out.
