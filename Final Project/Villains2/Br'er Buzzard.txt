He does not appear in [[Song of the South]] but he does appear in the comics based on the movie.\n\n==Song of the South==\n\nBr\u2019er Buzzard does not appear in the Song of the South movie but he does appear in the original uncle Remus stories.\n[[File:Old_Buzzard_and_Fox.jpg|thumb|Br\u2019er Buzzard and Br\u2019er Fox in the Uncle Remus stories]]\n\n==Comics==\n\nIn 1946 Disney made the first Br\u2019er Rabbit comics based of Song of the South. Disney added more Uncle Remus characters which did not appear in the movie. Br\u2019er Buzzard appears regularly in the Br\u2019er Rabbit comics. He is a member of the Foul Fellows Club and is often seen with Br\u2019er Fox and Br\u2019er Bear. They usually try to catch Br\u2019er Rabbit but they always fail. Unlike Br'er Fox, Br'er Bear and the Big Bad Wolf, Br'er Buzzard isn\u2019t trying to catch an animal to eet. He is most often seen stealing and deceiving Br'er Rabbit. In the first few comics he sometimes succeeds but in the newer comics he always fails. \n[[File:Foul_Fellows_Club.png|thumb|Br\u2019er Bear, Br\u2019er Buzzard, Br\u2019er Fox, Big Bad Wolf and Br\u2019er Weasel]]\n\n\n{{Song of the South}}\n{{CharacterStub}}\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Song of the South characters]]\n[[Category:Characters introduced in comics]]\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Vultures]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]"
