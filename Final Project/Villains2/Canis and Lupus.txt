"[[File:Canis_y_Lupus.jpg|thumb|250px]]'''Canis and Lupis''' are a pair of wicked winged wolves (also known as Empusa) who work for\u00a0[[Hecate]]. They appear in [[Hercules (TV series) |''Hercules'']]. They have wings and are usually black, although look blue in one scene.\n\nThey speak eloquently, but really are very vicious.\n\n== Trivia ==\n* When the names of the wolves are joined together, they form \"Canis lupus\", the scientific name of the wolf.\n* Lupus is actually the name for a condition, which mostly affects women that may bring on a rash. A [https://www.lupus.org/resources/what-is-lupus 'chronic autoimmune disease that can damage any part of the body']. Its full name is Systemic Lupus Erythematosus, the acronym being SLE. As of now, there are 4 types of Lupus. Unfortunately, it cannot be cured.  Possible conditions/symptoms could be [https://www.mayoclinic.org/diseases-conditions/lupus/symptoms-causes/syc-20365789 fever, fatigue, dry eyes, headaches, and lots more.] \n*It is possible they are inspired by the enfield, a fictional creature featured in heraldry that is sometimes portrayed as a winged wolf or dog.\n\n{{Hercules}}\n[[Category:Villains]]\n\n[[Category:Hercules characters]]\n[[Category:Monsters]]\n[[Category:Demons]]\n[[Category:Characters who fly]]\n[[Category:Henchmen]]\n[[Category:Character pairs]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Wolves]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]"
