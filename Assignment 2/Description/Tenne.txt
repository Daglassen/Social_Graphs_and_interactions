Tenne is a young female [[Hylian]] stable hand who can be found tending the hay troph at the [[South Akkala Stable]] in the [[Akkala Highlands]] region of [[Hyrule]] in the province of Akkala. She is the daughter of the stable's manager [[Dmitri]] while the other stable hands [[Kaifa]], [[Jana]], and [[Gleema]] are her sisters. She is one of four female stable hands that allows [[Link]] to style the mane and change the equipment of the registered horse he currently rides though only if they have bonded with him completely.
