"{{Infobox_character\n|image=Toy Duck.jpg\n|rides=[[Haunted Mansion Holiday]]\n|films=''[[The Nightmare Before Christmas]]''\n|games=''[[Disney Crossy Road]]''\n|designer=[[Tim Burton]]\n|voice=[[Greg Proops]]\n|alias=The Zombie Duck<br>The Bullet Hole Duck<br>The Toy Duck\n|status = Disappeared}}\n'''Toy Duck''' or '''Zombie Duck''' is the duck seen in [[Tim Burton]]'s ''[[The Nightmare Before Christmas]]''.\n\n==Appearances==\n\n===''The Nightmare Before Christmas''===\nThe Toy Duck was made during the \u00a8[[Making Christmas]]\u00a8 sequence to be one of [[Jack Skellington]]'s macabre [[Christmas]]  presents. The duck was created by the [[Vampires]] of [[Halloween Town]].\n\nJack Skellington gives him and a vampire teddy bear to a pair of children when he gives presents to the little kids in the real world. Near the end of the movie, [[Santa Claus]] comes and replaces the toy duck with a toy boat. He does the same with the other toys that Jack delivered.\n\n==Other Appearances==\n\n===[[Disney Crossy Road]]===\nThe toy duck is a playable character in this mobile-app based game.\n\n===[[Haunted Mansion Holiday]]===\nIn both versions of Haunted Mansion Holiday, you can see him in the attic scene along with other scary toys Jack delivered in the movie. He also appears in one of the portraits in the stretching room.\n\n==Trivia==\n*The Toy Duck shares a resemblance to [[Donald Duck]] as shown by the pajamas of the kid who received the toy\n*From 2001-2002, the duck toy appeared in the exit crypt of the [[Haunted Mansion Holiday]] along with Jack's other scary toys\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery>\nScary Christmas Toys.jpeg\nVampire Teddy with Duck POP.jpg\nHMH Stretch room portrait.jpg\nHaunted Mansion Holiday Attic Duck.jpg\nHaunted Mansion Holiday 2001 Exit Crypt.png\n</gallery>\n\n{{The Nightmare Before Christmas}}\n[[Category:The Nightmare Before Christmas characters]]\n[[Category:Ducks]]\n[[Category:Toys]]\n[[Category:Disney Crossy Road characters]]\n[[Category:Touchstone characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Characters in video games]]\n[[Category:Neutral characters]]\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Objects]]\n[[Category:The Haunted Mansion characters]]"
