While it cannot be defeated, it does not actively try to harm {{Term|BotW|Link|link}}. However, if {{Term|BotW|Link}} gets too close, he will be frozen by the icy aura surrounding its body. {{Term|BotW|Link}} can prevent being frozen by wearing either a full {{Term|BotW|Snowquill Set|link}} that has been enhanced twice by a {{Term|BotW|Great Fairy|link}}, or {{Term|BotW|Zant's Helmet|link}}. {{Term|BotW|Naydra}} will drop different {{Plural|BotW|Material|link}} if {{Term|BotW|Link}} shoots it with a {{Term|BotW|Bow|link}}. Depending on what part of {{Term|BotW|Naydra}} is hit, it will drop either a {{Term|BotW|Shard of Naydra's Horn|link}}, {{Term|BotW|Shard of Naydra's Fang|link}}, {{Term|BotW|Naydra's Claw|link}}, or {{Term|BotW|Naydra's Scale|link}}.\n\n{{Term|BotW|Naydra's Scale}} can be offered at the Spring of Wisdom to reveal the {{Term|BotW|Jitan Sa'mi Shrine|link}}.\n\n===Location===\n{{Term|BotW|Naydra}}, unlike the other dragons, does not roam Hyrule at first. It is first encountered as {{Term|BotW|Malice Naydra|link}} at the [[Spring of Wisdom]]. After {{Term|BotW|Link|link}} has purified {{Term|BotW|Naydra}} of the {{Term|BotW|Malice}}, {{Term|BotW|Naydra}} roams the {{Term|BotW|Lanayru (Region)|link}} and {{Term|BotW|East Necluda|link}} Regions.
