"[[File:Ranger Ron.png|thumb|250px]]\n'''Ranger Ron''' is a one-time villain from the [[Jetix]] series ''[[Yin Yang Yo!]]''.\n\n==History==\nHe is a pig who holds a grudge against bears for what they did to him ranging from a bear becoming his supervisor, a bear \"mauling him\" in a movie theatre, and bears causing him to go into a 12 item checkout lane at the grocery store when he had 13 items. He manipulated\u00a0Yin\u00a0and\u00a0Yang\u00a0into becoming bear in his plot to discredit all bears.
