"{{Infobox character\n|name = Black Dog\n|image = [[File:Black_Dog.jpg|thumb|250px]]\n|films = ''[[Muppet Treasure Island]]''<br>''[[The Muppets' Wizard of Oz]]''<br>''[[Muppets Most Wanted]]''\n|shows = ''Mopatop's Shop''\n|games = ''[[Muppet Treasure Island (video game)]]''\n|actor = [[Kevin Clash]]\n|alias = Penguin-Leg Pete (''Mopatop's Shop'')\n|personality = \n|appearance = \n|occupation = Pirate (''Muppet Treasure Island'')<br>Flying monkey (''The Muppets' Wizard of Oz'')<br>Gulag prisoner (''Muppets Most Wanted'')\n|alignment = Bad\n|goal = \n|home = \n|friends = [[Blind Pew]], [[Long John Silver (Muppets)|Long John Silver's]] pirates\n|enemies = [[Gonzo]], [[Rizzo the Rat|Rizzo]]\n|likes = \n|dislikes = \n|powers = None\n|weapons = \n|fate = \n|quote = \n}}'''Black Dog''' is a pirate in ''[[Muppet Treasure Island]]''.\n==History==\nAllied with Blind Pew, this vicious canine leads the raid on [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/The_Admiral_Benbow_Inn the Admiral Benbow Inn], in search of Billy Bones' map. A rather cruel pirate, he threatens Gonzo and Rizzo with threats of death. However, soon Black Dog and his men are fleeing with Gonzo and Rizzo from an ignited trail of gun powder. He eventually is cornered by the trail and is blown through the inn's roof by an explosion.\n\nHe also appeared as a member of the [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Flying_Monkeys Flying Monkeys] in ''[[The Muppets' Wizard of Oz]]'', as Penguin-Leg Pete in the ''Mopatop's Shop'' episode \"Penguins, Pegs, and Pirates\" and as a gulag prisoner in ''[[Muppets Most Wanted]]''.\n\n== Gallery ==\n<gallery widths=\"185\">\nBlack Dog.jpg\nBlack Dog Muppet.jpg\nBlack_Dog_Prisoner.webp\n</gallery>\n\n==Literary Source==\nIn the original ''[[Treasure Island]]'', Black Dog is the nickname of a pirate, \"a pale, tallowy creature, wanting two fingers of the left hand\" who \"was not sailorly, and yet he had a smack of the sea about him too.\" Preceding Blind Pew, he is the first to track Billy Bones to the Inn, meeting to discuss the issue of the map first, but Bones drives Black Dog out, \"in full flight... streaming blood from the left shoulder.\" The Muppet version takes the description literally, turning the character into a black-furred dog.\n\n{{MuppetsWiki}}\n\n{{The Muppets}}\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:The Muppets characters]]\n[[Category:Muppet Treasure Island characters]]\n[[Category:The Muppets' Wizard of Oz characters]]\n[[Category:Muppets Most Wanted characters]]\n[[Category:Pirates]]\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Henchmen]]\n[[Category:Dogs]]\n[[Category:Anthropomorphic characters]]\n[[Category:Live-action characters]]\n[[Category:Acquired characters]]\n[[Category:Characters in video games]]"
