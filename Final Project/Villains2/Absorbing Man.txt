'''Absorbing Man''', is a villain who can turn into any material he touches.\n\nAbsorbing Man was created by\u00a0[[Stan Lee]]\u00a0and [[Jack Kirby]].\n\n==Appearances==\n===Marvel Animated Universe===\n====''[[The Avengers: Earth's Mightiest Heroes]]''====\nBruce Banner seeks Creel to find information about the Cube, the prison for Gamma irradiated villains. However, Creel absorbs the metal of a spoon and attacks Banner to provoke him so he can turn into the [[Hulk]] and fight him. Creel is defeated and sent to the Cube.\n\nDuring the breakout, Absorbing Man and [[Abomination]] helped the [[Leader]] take over the Cube.\n\nWhen the Leader activated his Gamma dome in Las Vegas, Absorbing Man absorbed [[Mjolnir]] to fight [[Thor Odinson|Thor]], but this resulted on his undoing as Thor merely concentrated in order to control him just like he controls Mjolnir, so he sent Absorbing Man crashing into the Leader.\n\nAbsorbing Man was sent to Prison 42, and was one of the prisoners who helped the Avengers when [[Annihilus]] invaded the prison.\n\n====''[[Hulk and the Agents of S.M.A.S.H.]]''====\nAbsorbing Man, Titania, and the [[Wrecking Crew]] escaped the Cube and soon faced Hulk's team. \u00a0He is the boyfriend of Titania.\n\n===[[Marvel Cinematic Universe]]===\n====''[[Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.]]''====\nCarl Creel is a former boxer was recruited by [[John Garrett]] to be the new [[HYDRA]] assassin. He was sent by HYDRA to recover the Obelisk. In that mission, he attacks Glenn Talbot to know the whereabouts of the [[Obelisk]]. However, his plans were ruined because it was captured by Melinda May, Skye, and the military.\n\nWhile he was in prison of the militia, Creel uses his powers and manages to blend into his cell, believing that the prisoner escaped the guards enter in the cell. Creel hits them and escapes and goes in search of the Obelisk. When Creel finds the Obelisk, he sees that this object begins to petrify Agent Hartley, seeing this Creel decides to flee. Later, he attacks the van which held [[Lance Hunter]] and [[Isabelle Hartley]]. Absorbing Man uses his powers to petrify himself and thus gets the Obelisk.\n\n====''[[Daredevil (TV series)|Daredevil]]''====\nCarl Creel is mentioned, in a flashback, where he was set to box with Jack Murdock.\n\n==Trivia==\n*In the comics, Carl Creel received his powers from [[Loki Laufeyson|Loki]] to confront Thor.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery spacing=\"small\" position=\"center\" widths=\"150\" captionalign=\"center\">\nAgents of S.H.I.E.L.D.
