In Morag, they meet Peter Quill, who presented as Star-Lord, Korath said he had never heard of him and would take him with Ronan. Peter gives them the [[Orb]] but he uses a distraction from Korath and the Sakaarans and recover the Orb.\n\nKorath and his army of Sakaarans chase him but Peter gets up to Milano and escapes. Later, the Sakaarans accompanying Ronan to [[Knowhere]], where they get the Orb.
