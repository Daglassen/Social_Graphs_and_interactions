"[[File:Scythe.jpg|thumb]]'''Scythe''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character who appears in ''[[The Avengers: Earth's Mightiest Heroes]]'', and in ''[[Iron Fist (TV series)|Iron Fist]]'', played by David Sakurai. He is a mercenary who is often hired by supervillains to kill superheroes. His supervillain codename comes from his weapon of choice, a razor-sharp scythe. He is a master of several different kinds of martial arts.\n\nIn ''Earth's Mightiest Heroes'', Crossfire hires him to kill [[Hank Pym]].
