After her father left her mother, Dorothy Walker began putting her daughter through acting school, which would later land Patricia the role in the TV show \"It's Patsy\", making her the most famous child star in the world at the time. For a publicity move, Patricia's mother adopts a classmate of Patricia's who has been in a car accident, making [[Jessica Jones]] her adoptive sister.\n\nIn her adult years, Patricia reinvents herself as \"Trish Walker\", trying to wash away her teen star years, running a talk show by the name of ''Trish Talk''.\n\nLater, she injects herself with chemicals that give him superhuman strength, superhuman speed and sharp nails, which she uses to help Jessica fight crime.\n\n===Personality===\nTrish is endlessly supportive and a true friend.
