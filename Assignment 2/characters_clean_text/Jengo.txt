Jengo is a character in Breath of the Wild.


== Biography ==
Jengo is a Goron miner who works at the Southern Mine in the Eldin Canyon. During the day, Jengo mines alongside Dorill and Greyson for Gems. At night, Jengo takes his break while Bohrin works the night shift.
Jengo is focused on his work, and prefers not to be disturbed until he is on his break. During break, Jengo mentions that the Gorons used to mine at the Death Mountain Summit before the Divine Beast Vah Rudania appeared, forcing them to mine at the Southern Mine. He tells Link to speak with Bludo, the boss of the Gorons, for more information.
