"{{Infobox character\n|image = Hex.jpg\n|games = ''[[Disney Universe]]<br>[[Disney Emoji Blitz]]''\n|voice = [[Bob Bergen]]\n|personality = Evil, arrogant, cocky, cunning, manipulative, idiotic\n|appearance = Flexing black cube, sinister red eyes and mouth\n|alignment = Bad\n|goal = To take over the Disney Universe\n|home = The Disney Universe\n|minions = [[Bots]]\n|enemies = [[VIC]]\n|likes = Chaos\n|dislikes = His plans being interfered\n|fate = Sings a rock version of ''[[It's a Small World (song)|It's a Small World]]'' and leaves\n|quote = \"I am HEX the boss of bosses.\"<br>\"Welcome to my Universe!\"}}'''HEX''' is the main villain of the video game ''[[Disney Universe]]''.\n\n==Role in the video game==\n===Introduction / Finale Cutscenes===\nHe is first seen in the intro for the game where he is seen taking over Disney Universe when VIC notices some Bots attacking guests. Later during the end of the introduction cutscene, he explains that he now rules Disney Universe as well as taking over [[VIC]].\n\nOnce the player completes all six worlds at once, VIC ends up confronting HEX for trying to cause more chaos in the Disney Universe where he plans to take revenge on the guests at the Disney Universe. When VIC asks HEX to leave, he then tells VIC that he will perform ''[[It's a Small World (song)|It's a Small World]]'', but he decides to perform said song despite VIC's protests thinking he might cause more chaos again.\n\nHEX is last seen singing a rock version of ''It's a Small World'' before he leaves, the park retaken from him.\n\n===In the levels===\nIn multiplayer, HEX power-ups can be used which includes a bomb head, a boot, a basketball and a chicken; these power-ups can be used to help the player take out his or her opponents specifically to take more gold from them. He is also seen at the start of each level during the loading screen mocking VIC's tips and assistance.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Despite being the main villain in the game, HEX does not appear as a fightable boss in any of the levels.\n[[Category:Disney Universe]]\n[[Category:Characters in video games]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Singing characters]]\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Objects]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Characters introduced in video games]]\n[[Category:Disney Interactive Studios characters]]\n[[Category:Main antagonists]]"
