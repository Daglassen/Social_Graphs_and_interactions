He is the ruler of Dream World and uses his sleep sand to put others to sleep. He only appeared in the episode \"[[Ghoul of My Dreams]]\".\n\n==Role in the episode==\nIn order to obtain enough money to pay off her student loan, [[Morgana Macawber]] struck a deal with Nodoff. He would help her out by using his sleep sand to lull the denizens of [[St. Canard]] to sleep.
