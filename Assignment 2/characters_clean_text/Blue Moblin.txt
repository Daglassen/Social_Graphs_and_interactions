Blue Moblins are recurring Enemies in The Legend of Zelda series.[name reference needed]


== Characteristics ==


=== The Legend of Zelda ===
Blue Moblins first appear in The Legend of Zelda, being seen in forest areas like the Lost Woods. They act the same as Moblins, but can withstand more hits.


=== The Adventure of Link ===
Blue Moblins later reappear in Zelda II: The Adventure of Link, being the strongest Moblin in the game. Like the other Moblins in The Adventure of Link, they attack by throwing their spears or jabbing Link with them, while also draining his experience points if they land a hit on him.

=== Breath of the Wild ===

Reappearing for the first time since the Oracle games, Blue Moblins are found all over Hyrule and are now tall and heavy, similar to the normal Moblins. They are tougher variants of Moblins and often have stronger Moblin Weapons, though they are now outclassed by Black Moblins and Silver Moblins. When defeated, they drop Moblin Horns, Moblin Fangs, and Moblin Guts.


=== Sequel to Breath of the Wild ===

Blue Moblins are set to reappear in Sequel to Breath of the Wild.
