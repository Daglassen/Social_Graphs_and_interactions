She appears in the ''[[DuckTales]]'' episode \"[[The Good Muddahs]]\" together with her sisters \"[[Bouffant Beagle]]\" [767-171] and \"[[Babydoll Beagle]]\" [776-177].\n\n==Personality==\nShe has a somewhat ditzy, childlike personality and seems somewhat naive. She can use her \"charm\" to distract people, so her sisters can rob them. While the Beagle Babes are thieves, they don't seem to be very evil in nature.
