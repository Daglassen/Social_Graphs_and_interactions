Zorona is a character in Breath of the Wild.


== Biography ==
Zorona is a traveler who can be found riding his Horse along the shore near Zelo Pond in Eldin Canyon. He is a self-proclaimed mystery buff, and is curious about the mysteries in the areas nearby. However, Zorona is much more curious about the rumors of the Fang and Bone shop, and has been desperately trying to learn more about it. He deduces that its location is at the Skull Lake north of Death Mountain, but also mentions that it has been spotted in other places too.
Zorona also mentions other mysteries that he has heard or claims to have solved. He claims that there is legendary equipment found in Hyrule Castle and Korok Forest. He also claims to have found hidden Koroks.
