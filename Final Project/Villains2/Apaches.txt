They are a tribe of unscrupulous Indians who have in their number one slightly wiser Comanche Chief.\n\n==Role in the film==\n[[Travis Coates|Travis]], [[Arliss Coates|Arliss]], and [[Lisbeth Searcy|Lisbeth]] are taken captive by Apaches stealing horses. Travis is left behind when the Apaches are chased by a cavalry patrol and is found by [[Savage Sam (character)|Savage Sam]] (thanks to his keen sense of smell).\n\nBeck gathers up a search party including [[Bud Searcy]] and neighbor, Wiley Crup.
