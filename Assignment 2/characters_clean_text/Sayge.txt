Sayge is a character in Breath of the Wild.


== Biography ==
Sayge is the manager of the Kochi Dye Shop in Hateno Village. He is an eccentric fellow with dark red hair that appears to be dyed, and is styled in an undercut fashion. He is the husband of Senna. He has a propensity for making color-based puns.


== Nomenclature ==
Sayge could be named after the Sage plant, a plant used in spices and dyes.
