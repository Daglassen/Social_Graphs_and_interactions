The ruthless, merciless and cruel god of war, he is an enemy of [[Hercules (Marvel)|Hercules]] and [[Avengers|the Avengers]]. He is an expert at hand-to-hand combat and has mastered both the sword and the axe as his preferred weapons in combat.\n\nAres tried to steal the Key of Tartarus away from Hercules, but thankfully Hercules and the Avengers managed to defeat Ares.
