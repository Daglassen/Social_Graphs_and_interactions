While it cannot be defeated, it does not actively try to harm {{Term|BotW|Link|link}}. However, if {{Term|BotW|Link}} gets too close, he will be damaged by the electricity surrounding its body, unless he possesses a full set of [[Rubber Armor]] that has been enhanced twice by a {{Term|BotW|Great Fairy|link}} or the [[Thunder Helm]] for the [[Unshockable]] [[Set Bonus]]. {{Term|BotW|Farosh}} will drop different {{Plural|BotW|Material|link}} if {{Term|BotW|Link}} shoots it with a {{Term|BotW|Bow|link}}. Depending on where {{Term|BotW|Farosh}} is shot, it will drop a [[Shard of Farosh's Horn]], [[Shard of Farosh's Fang]], [[Farosh's Claw]] or [[Farosh's Scale]].
