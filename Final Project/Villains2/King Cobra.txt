He can form a metallic tail from his belt which covers his legs.\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[The Avengers: Earth's Mightiest Heroes]]''===\nDuring the\u00a0[http://avengersearthsmightiestheroes.wikia.com/wiki/Breakout_(Event) Breakout], he broke out of the\u00a0[http://avengersearthsmightiestheroes.wikia.com/wiki/The_Big_House Big House]\u00a0and tried to attack\u00a0[[Henry Pym|Hank Pym]]. Hank Pym managed to escape from his grasp and escape. Later, he and the Serpent Society battled the Avengers in a subway.\n\nUnfortunately, Hank Pym's pacifist decisions cause the\u00a0[http://avengersearthsmightiestheroes.wikia.com/wiki/Serpent_Society Serpent Society]\u00a0to escape.\n\nEventually, he was captured by the\u00a0[http://avengersearthsmightiestheroes.wikia.com/wiki/Skrull Skrulls]\u00a0and replaced with\u00a0[http://avengersearthsmightiestheroes.wikia.com/wiki/King_Cobra_(Skrull) an impostor]. When\u00a0[[Captain America]]\u00a0and\u00a0[[Viper|Madame Hydra]]\u00a0led an escape from the Skrull spaceship, he joined them and fought against the [[Super Skrull]].\n\nDuring the battle in\u00a0Washington, D.C., King Cobra helped the Avengers against the Skrulls.
