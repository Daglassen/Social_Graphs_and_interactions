He puts them through a series of tests, mostly through electroshock therapy in response to visual identifiers.\n\nThrough his research though, he has discovered that the\u00a0Corsican fruit\u00a0has a bizarre effect on twins, allowing them to experience each other's feelings and emotions. As part of his experiment, he gives\u00a0Zack\u00a0and Cody the fruit, as well as the other members of the Gemini Project.\n\nWhen Zack and Cody go through his office, they learn that Dr. Olsen in fact is not working in their best interests, and is using the Corsican fruit to create a hive mind of twins, all under his control. Though he is unable to brainwash Zack and Cody, he sends hundreds of other twins to capture them so that his hive mind can be complete, he was\u00a0revealed to be Donald's evil twin brother who\u00a0disappeared when the scicetific community threw him out and When he attempts to merge the twins, much to the dismay of\u00a0Mr. Moseby,\u00a0Bailey,\u00a0London, and\u00a0Woody, they continue to fight and the machines designed to run the project are destroyed which sets free the other twins from its control.\n\nOnce Dr. Olsen begins to threaten everyone, Bailey uses a lasso to catch him until the police can arrive to arrest him. the Sapulding twins actullay\u00a0wanted to be like each other all along and promise to meet after Ronald is released from prison.\n\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery>\nThe Spaulding Twins.png\n</gallery>\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n{{Suite Life}}\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Live-action villains]]\n[[Category:The Suite Life characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Scientists]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Twins and Triplets]]\n[[Category:Reformed characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Siblings]]\n[[Category:Main antagonists]]\n[[Category:Disney Channel characters]]"
