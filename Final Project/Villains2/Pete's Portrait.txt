"[[File:IMG_9080.jpg|thumb|250px]]\n'''Pete's Portrait''' is an antagonist who appears in the video game ''[[The Great Circus Mystery starring Mickey and Minnie|The Great Circus Mystery]]'' as the mini-boss of the Haunted House level. The painting is of [[Pete]] as an emperor. Once the player walks closer, the portrait comes to life and begins to move.
